Name: Squid
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Aquatic

Health: 10 HP (5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Swims at ~0.20
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Oceans (all non-frozen variants)
- Rivers
- Underwater in any depth

Drops:
- Ink Sac (1-3)

Special Abilities:
- Squirts ink when attacked (visual effect only — no damage or slowness)
- Swims in all directions (unique fluid 3D movement)
- Suffocates on land (takes damage outside water)

Ink Sac Uses:
- Black Dye (crafting)
- Writing in Books (ink)
- Printing text in game signs
- Crafting Dark-color items

Behavior:
- Swims aimlessly in 3D space through water
- Moves away from attacks
- Squirts harmless ink cloud when hurt

Sounds: Squirting sounds (when hit)

Notes:
- Simple but iconic mob — one of the oldest aquatic mobs
- Ink Sacs are used in Book and Quill (written books) and Black Dye
- Glow Squid variant (added in 1.17) has a special glowing version
- Squids can be pushed by flowing water currents
- Cannot survive out of water — suffocates on land

Name: Zombie Villager
Type: Hostile
Category: Undead

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: 3 (Easy) / 4 (Normal) / 6 (Hard)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.23
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Overworld (night, light level 0)
- When a Zombie kills a Villager (100% Hard, 50% Normal, 0% Easy)
- Zombie Villages (all villagers are zombified)

Drops:
- Rotten Flesh (0-2)
- Iron Ingot (rare)
- Carrot / Potato (rare)
- Villager profession item (rare)

Special Abilities:
- Can be cured into a regular Villager:
  1. Throw Splash Potion of Weakness at it
  2. Feed it a Golden Apple
  3. Wait 2-5 minutes while it shakes/emits red particles
  → Becomes a Villager with deeply discounted trades (1 emerald for many items)
- Burns in sunlight (unless helmeted)
- Retains Villager profession visuals (looks like a zombie with robes)

Weaknesses:
- Smite enchantment
- Sunlight
- Golden Apple + Weakness potion (cures it)

Immunities:
- Poison (undead)

Behavior:
- Same as Zombie — chases players, avoids sunlight
- Can be cured which makes it extremely valuable

Sounds: Zombie sounds with occasional villager "hm" noises

Notes:
- Curing a Zombie Villager is one of the best investments in the game
- Cured villagers give massive discounts (often 1 emerald) for all their trades
- Zombie Villages are rare but all their villagers can be cured for a cheap trading hall
- Zombie Villagers retain their profession outfit even as zombies

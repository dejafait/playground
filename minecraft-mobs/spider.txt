Name: Spider
Type: Neutral (hostile in darkness, neutral in light)
Category: Arthropod

Health: 16 HP (8 hearts)
Attack Damage: 2 (Easy) / 2 (Normal) / 3 (Hard)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.30
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Overworld (night, light level 0)
- Dark caves
- Spider spawners in dungeons

Drops:
- String (0-2)
- Spider Eye (1/3 chance)

Special Abilities:
- Can climb any vertical surface (wall climbing)
- Wider than 1 block (2 blocks wide) — can't fit through 1-block gaps
- Jumps at players to attack
- Neutral in daytime or sufficient light (does not attack unless provoked)
- Can spawn with potion effects from spawners (speed, strength, etc.)
- See through walls using special AI pathfinding

Weaknesses:
- Smite enchantment does NOT work (not undead)
- Bane of Arthropods enchantment deals extra damage and slows
- Light prevents natural spawning

Immunities:
- Fall damage resistance (web climbing behavior)

Behavior:
- Wanders at night, attacks players on sight
- Passive during daylight unless attacked
- Climbs walls and ceilings to pursue player
- Leaps forward to attack

Sounds: Hissing, skittering

Notes:
- Can carry a Skeleton on its back (Spider Jockey — 1% chance)
- 2 blocks wide so it cannot enter standard 1-block doorways
- One of the original Minecraft mobs
- Useful for farming String (bows, fishing rods) and Spider Eyes (potions)

Name: Iron Golem
Type: Neutral (hostile to threats of villagers/player)
Category: Utility / Construct

Health: 100 HP (50 hearts)
Attack Damage: 11 (Easy) / 7-21 range (Normal) / 21 (Hard) + extreme knockback
Armor: 0 (natural resistance)
Movement Speed: 0.25
XP Dropped: 0

Spawn Locations:
- Villages (natural spawn when village has 10+ villagers and 21+ houses/beds)
- Player-built: 4 Iron Blocks in T-shape + Carved Pumpkin/Jack o'Lantern on top
- Iron Golem farms (artificial spawning conditions)

Drops:
- Iron Ingot (3-5)
- Poppy (0-2)

Special Abilities:
- Attacks all hostile mobs that threaten players or villagers
- Extreme knockback — launches mobs and players high into the air
- Immune to drowning, fall damage
- Can hold and offer Poppies to baby villagers (passive idle behavior)
- Health is visually shown through cracks on the body (4 crack stages)
- Can be healed with Iron Ingots (25 HP per ingot — right-click)
- Player-built golems are neutral to players
- Village-spawned golems may attack players with very low village reputation

Weaknesses:
- Slow movement speed
- Fire (not immune — deals damage)
- Can be griefed if village reputation is very negative (attacks player)

Immunities:
- Fall damage
- Drowning

Behavior:
- Patrols village area
- Attacks hostile mobs on sight
- Offers Poppies to baby villagers
- Attacks players with very low reputation (from harming villagers)
- Neutral to player if built by player (not village-spawned)

Sounds: Heavy metallic clanging footsteps, angry groan when attacked

Notes:
- One of the most powerful non-boss mobs in the game
- Iron Golem farms are one of the best passive iron sources
- Originally inspired by a Studio Ghibli robot (Castle in the Sky)
- Village reputation system means murdering villagers will turn their Golems against you
- Introduced as a "community mob" in Beta 1.9 — players petitioned for a defensive mob

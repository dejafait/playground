Name: Hoglin
Type: Hostile (passive in safe zones)
Category: Nether / Animal

Health: 40 HP (20 hearts)
Attack Damage: 6 (Easy) / 10 (Normal) / 15 (Hard) + knockback
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.30
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Crimson Forest (Nether)
- Bastion Remnants

Drops:
- Raw Porkchop (2-4, or Cooked Porkchop if on fire)
- Leather (0-1)

Special Abilities:
- Knocks player/mobs backward on hit (significant knockback)
- Can be bred with Crimson Fungus
- Transforms into Zoglin when entering the Overworld or End
- Hunted and killed by Piglins (Piglins celebrate after killing one)
- Avoids and flees from: Warped Fungus, Nether Portals, Respawn Anchors

Weaknesses:
- Warped Fungus — Hoglins flee from it (use to keep them at bay)
- Nether Portals and Respawn Anchors (area of safety around these)
- Piglins will hunt and kill them

Immunities:
- Fire and lava

Behavior:
- Attacks players and most non-Piglin mobs on sight
- Passive only when within range of Warped Fungus / portals
- Baby Hoglins exist and are passive
- Can be bred to create a Hoglin farm (porkchop source in Nether)

Sounds: Grunting, squealing when hurt, snorting

Notes:
- One of the best food sources in the Nether (drops cooked porkchops if on fire)
- Placing Warped Fungus near you keeps Hoglins away
- One of the few Nether mobs that can be farmed for food
- Transforms into Zoglin immediately upon entering Overworld/End
- Baby Hoglins make excellent passive companions (never hostile)

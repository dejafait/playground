Name: Horse
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Rideable

Health: 15-30 HP (7.5-15 hearts, randomized per individual)
Attack Damage: None (doesn't attack — though wild horse will kick nearby mobs occasionally)
Armor: Up to 20 (with Horse Armor equipped)
Movement Speed: 0.1125-0.3375 (varies per individual — significant range)
Jump Height: 1.5-5.5 blocks (varies per individual)
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Plains
- Savanna
- Spawn in herds of 2-6

Coat Variants (35 visual combinations):
5 base colors × 7 marking patterns:
Colors: White, Creamy, Chestnut, Brown, Black, Gray, Dark Brown
Markings: None, White Socks, White Field, White Dots, Black Dots

Taming:
- Right-click to ride (no items needed)
- Will buck player off repeatedly until tamed (random chance increases with each attempt)
- Feed (apples, wheat, sugar, bread, hay bales) to speed up taming
- Once tamed, put saddle in its inventory to steer

Equipment Slots:
- Saddle (required to steer/ride)
- Horse Armor (Iron, Gold, Diamond, or Leather — does NOT break, is permanent)

Special Abilities:
- Fastest land transportation in the base game
- Can jump over fences (1.5-5.5 block jump varies by horse — breed for best)
- Can be bred with Golden Apple or Golden Carrot (two tamed horses)
- Baby foals exist and can be tamed when grown
- Speed and jump height are inherited/averaged from parents (breeding optimization)

Behavior:
- Wanders plains and savannas
- Follows players holding Golden Apples/Carrots
- Bred horses inherit stats from parents

Sounds: Neighing, clip-clop hooves, snorting

Notes:
- Horses have 3 stats: Speed, Jump Height, and HP — all randomized and inheritable
- Breeding selects the best horses iteratively (selective breeding for speed/jump)
- Diamond Horse Armor provides the best protection (20 armor points)
- Horses can't be ridden underwater effectively (swimming horse is very slow)
- Fastest horse possible (maximum speed) is noticeably faster than Boots of Speed potion

Name: Wolf
Type: Neutral (tamed = passive to owner)
Category: Animal

Health: 8 HP (wild) / 20 HP (tamed)
Attack Damage: 4 (wild) / 4-6 (tamed — increases with player's attack attribute)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.30
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Forest
- Taiga
- Giant Tree Taiga
- Snowy Taiga

Drops:
- Nothing (0 XP if killed by player; 1-3 if killed by other mob)

Taming:
- Tame with Bones (random chance per bone — roughly 1/3)
- Red collar appears when tamed
- Collar color can be changed with any dye

Variants (added 1.21+):
- Pale Wolf (Taiga — default white)
- Ashen Wolf (Snowy Taiga — grey)
- Black Wolf (Old Growth Pine Taiga — dark)
- Chestnut Wolf (Old Growth Spruce Taiga — brown)
- Rusty Wolf (Sparse Jungle — orange)
- Spotted Wolf (Savanna — dotted)
- Striped Wolf (Wooded Badlands — striped)
- Snowy Wolf (Grove — white with blue eyes)
- Woods Wolf (Forest — earthy brown)

Special Abilities:
- Tamed wolves follow and protect the owner
- Attack mobs that attack the owner (or are attacked by the owner)
- Naturally attack Skeletons, Strays, and Wither Skeletons
- Sit/Stand command (right-click)
- Can be healed with any meat
- Wolf Armor (crafted from Armadillo Scutes) can be equipped on tamed wolves
- Multiple wolves can be tamed and form a pack

Behavior:
- Wild wolves hunt and attack Sheep, Foxes, and Rabbits
- Wild wolves attack players who attack them (call for help from nearby wolves)
- Tamed wolves sit when told and follow player when standing
- Shake dry after being in water

Sounds: Barking, whimpering, growling

Notes:
- One of the oldest and most beloved companion mobs
- Wolf Armor (1.21) greatly increases their survivability
- Tamed wolves are one of the best early-game combat companions
- Multiple wolf variants added based on biome in 1.21
- Wolves automatically attack Skeletons — helpful in skeleton-heavy areas

Name: Llama
Type: Neutral (spits when provoked)
Category: Animal / Pack Animal

Health: 15-30 HP (varies, 7.5-15 hearts)
Attack Damage: Spit — 1 damage (harmless)
Armor: 0 (carpet decoration, no armor value)
Movement Speed: 0.175
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Savanna Plateau
- Windswept Hills (Extreme Hills)
- Windswept Forest
- Windswept Gravelly Hills
- Spawn in herds of 4-6

Drops:
- Leather (0-2)

Taming:
- Right-click to ride repeatedly (like horse — random chance, no item needed)
- Must tame before attaching chest or carpet

Strength Variants (determines chest capacity):
- Strength 1: 3 inventory slots
- Strength 2: 6 inventory slots
- Strength 3: 9 inventory slots
- Strength 4: 12 inventory slots
- Strength 5: 15 inventory slots

Equipment:
- Chest: Gives inventory slots based on Strength stat
- Carpet: Decorative pattern on back (color based on carpet color — 16 options)

Caravan Mechanic:
- Leash a Llama with a Lead → following llamas automatically link into a caravan (up to 10)
- Used by Wandering Traders (who walk with 2 Trader Llamas in caravan)

Special Abilities:
- Spits at hostile wolves (and players who attack them)
- Forms caravans when one is leashed
- High-volume storage when multiple Llamas follow in caravan
- Decorative carpet adds visual personality

Breeding:
- Feed 2 tamed Llamas Hay Bales

Behavior:
- Wanders mountains and savanna
- Spits at wolves and players who attack
- Baby llamas exist (cute but also spit)

Sounds: Humming, spitting

Notes:
- Best pack animals for large item transport (15 slots × 10 caravan = 150 item slots)
- Cannot be steered — they just follow the leading Llama or player
- Trader Llamas wear blue/cyan carpet (distinguishes them from wild Llamas)
- Spitting is largely harmless but funny
- Added as part of the 1.11 (Exploration) update

Name: Husk
Type: Hostile
Category: Undead

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: 3 (Easy) / 4 + Hunger II for 7s (Normal) / 6 + Hunger II for 15s (Hard)
Armor: 2
Movement Speed: 0.23
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Desert biomes exclusively (surface spawning during day and night)

Drops:
- Rotten Flesh (0-2)
- Carrot (rare, 2.5%)
- Potato (rare, 2.5%)
- Iron Ingot (rare, 2.5%)
- Equipped items (rare)

Special Abilities:
- Does NOT burn in sunlight — unique among undead humanoids
- Inflicts Hunger status effect on hit (drains food bar)
- If submerged in water long enough, converts to a Zombie
- Can pick up and equip items
- Breaks down doors on Hard difficulty (like Zombie)

Weaknesses:
- Water causes conversion to Zombie (not instant death)
- Smite enchantment (undead)
- Standard weapons

Immunities:
- Sunlight (does not burn)

Behavior:
- Wanders the desert during day and night
- Attacks players, villagers, and iron golems on sight
- Behaves like a Zombie but spawns in and tolerates sunlight
- Converts to Zombie when in water

Sounds: Same as Zombie — groaning

Notes:
- The desert variant of the Zombie
- The most dangerous aspect is the Hunger effect — drains food quickly and prevents sprinting
- Husks wandering in daylight in deserts are a surprising threat for new players
- Can turn into a Zombie and then a Drowned if submerged in water long enough

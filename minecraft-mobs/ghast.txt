Name: Ghast
Type: Hostile
Category: Nether Monster

Health: 10 HP (5 hearts)
Attack Damage: Fireball explosion — up to 17 (direct hit), varies with distance
Explosion Power: 1 (fireball) — but creates fire
Armor: 0
Size: 4x4x4 blocks (very large)
Movement Speed: 0.40 (flies)
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Nether (all biomes except Basalt Deltas — more rare there)
- Requires 5x4x5 space to spawn

Drops:
- Ghast Tear (0-1)
- Gunpowder (0-2)

Special Abilities:
- Large flying mob that fires explosive fireballs
- Fireballs can be deflected back by hitting them (sword, fist, bow, snowball)
- Reflected fireball kills Ghast in one hit
- Has a long range — can attack from very far away
- Opens eyes and makes a pre-attack sound before firing
- Cries when idle — one of the only mobs with "emotional" sounds

Weaknesses:
- Deflected fireballs (instant kill)
- Arrows — difficult to hit but effective
- Very low health despite its size

Immunities:
- Fire
- Lava

Behavior:
- Drifts slowly through the Nether
- Opens and closes its eyes (idle vs. targeting)
- Fires fireballs at players in range
- Makes crying sounds when not attacking

Sounds: Crying (idle), screaming (death), explosion

Notes:
- Ghast Tears are required for Potions of Regeneration and End Crystals
- One of the most iconic Nether mobs
- Despite being terrifying, it has very low HP — it just has huge range and firepower
- Deflecting a fireball back to kill a Ghast grants the "Return to Sender" achievement
- The crying sounds were recorded by C418's sleeping cat

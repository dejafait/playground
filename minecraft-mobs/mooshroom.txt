Name: Mooshroom (Mooshrrom)
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Variant

Health: 10 HP (5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.20
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Mushroom Fields biome (the rare mushroom island biome) ONLY
- Does not spawn anywhere else naturally

Variants:
- Red Mooshroom: Common — covered in red mushrooms
- Brown Mooshroom: Rare — spawns when Red Mooshroom is struck by lightning within 4 blocks

Drops:
- Leather (0-2)
- Raw Beef (1-3, Cooked Beef if on fire)
- Red Mushroom (5, when Red Mooshroom is sheared) — then becomes a normal Cow
- Brown Mushroom (5, when Brown Mooshroom is sheared) — then becomes a normal Cow

Special Abilities:
- Milked with Bucket → Milk (same as regular Cow)
- Milked with Bowl → Mushroom Stew (renewable, infinite food source with bowl)
- Brown Mooshroom milked with Bowl → Suspicious Stew (random potion effect based on flower fed to it)
- Shearing converts it into a regular Cow + drops 5 Mushrooms
- Brown Mooshroom: Feed it a flower before milking → that flower determines Suspicious Stew effect

Suspicious Stew Effects (Brown Mooshroom — based on flower fed):
- Dandelion: Saturation
- Poppy: Night Vision
- Blue Orchid: Saturation (long)
- Allium: Fire Resistance
- Azure Bluet: Blindness
- Red Tulip: Weakness
- Orange Tulip: Nausea
- White Tulip: Poison
- Pink Tulip: Weakness
- Oxeye Daisy: Regeneration
- Cornflower: Jump Boost
- Lily of the Valley: Poison

Behavior:
- Same as regular Cow (wanders peacefully)
- Only found on Mushroom Islands
- Mushroom Island biome is passive — no hostile mobs spawn there (except Phantoms for sleepless players)

Sounds: Mooing (same as Cow)

Notes:
- Mushroom Island is one of the safest biomes in the game — no hostile mob spawning
- Infinite Mushroom Stew from a Mooshroom + Bowl is one of the best early infinite food sources
- Brown Mooshroom + right flowers = powerful Suspicious Stew effects (Saturation especially valuable)
- Extremely rare biome but incredibly valuable if found early game

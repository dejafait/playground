Name: Salmon
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Aquatic

Health: 3 HP (1.5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Swims fast
XP Dropped: 1-3

Size Variants:
- Small (most common)
- Normal
- Large (rarest)

Spawn Locations:
- Cold Ocean
- Deep Cold Ocean
- Frozen Ocean
- Deep Frozen Ocean
- Rivers
- Frozen River

Drops:
- Raw Salmon (1)
- Bone Meal (rarely)
- Cooked Salmon (if on fire)

Special Abilities:
- Swims upstream in rivers (like real salmon)
- Can be caught in Water Bucket
- 3 size variants with identical behavior but different models
- Stronger than Cod in terms of food value when cooked

Salmon Item:
- Raw Salmon: 2 hunger bars, 0.4 saturation
- Cooked Salmon: 6 hunger bars, 9.6 saturation — one of the best cooked fish items
- Used to tame Cats (along with Cod)
- Ocelot trust earned with Salmon
- Fed to Dolphins (treasure hunting trigger)
- Polar Bears may drop Salmon

Behavior:
- Schools in rivers and cold oceans
- Jumps waterfalls and upstream current (like real salmon migration)

Sounds: Bubbling, splashing

Notes:
- Cooked Salmon has higher saturation than Cooked Cod — better food item
- The upstream swimming behavior accurately mimics real-world salmon migration
- Cold biome indicator — if you see Salmon, you're near cold waters
- 3 size variants are purely cosmetic with no mechanical difference
- One of the best fish to farm for food due to high cooked saturation value

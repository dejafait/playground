Name: Bogged
Type: Hostile
Category: Undead

Health: 16 HP (8 hearts)
Attack Damage: Poison Arrow — 2-4 + Poison II for 4 seconds (Normal)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.25
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Swamp biomes
- Mangrove Swamp biomes
- Trial Chambers (spawned by Trial Spawners)

Drops:
- Bones (0-2)
- Arrows (0-2)
- Tipped Arrow of Poison (50% chance instead of regular arrow)
- Bow (8.5%)
- Mushrooms (Brown and Red, rarely — due to mushroom-covered appearance)

Special Abilities:
- Shoots Tipped Arrows of Poison — inflicts Poison II for 4 seconds
- Burns in sunlight (like all skeleton variants)
- Lower HP than regular Skeleton (16 vs 20)
- Mushroom/moss-covered appearance
- Fires slower than a regular skeleton but with the Poison debuff compensation

Weaknesses:
- Sunlight (burns)
- Smite enchantment (undead)
- Lower HP makes it easier to kill than regular Skeleton

Immunities:
- Poison (the status effect itself)

Behavior:
- Same as Skeleton — strafe and shoot from range
- More dangerous in swamps where terrain limits player movement
- Burns at sunrise

Sounds: Skeleton bone sounds

Notes:
- Added in 1.21 (Tricky Trials update)
- Swamp/wetland variant of the Skeleton
- Mushroom-covered body gives it a distinctive look
- Lower health than regular skeletons — trades durability for the Poison arrow effect
- Poison effect from Tipped Arrows stacks with other damage and drains health over time

Name: Guardian
Type: Hostile
Category: Aquatic Monster

Health: 30 HP (15 hearts)
Attack Damage: Laser 6 (charges over 2 seconds) / Thorns 2 (if player melee attacks)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Swims at ~0.30
XP Dropped: 10

Spawn Locations:
- Ocean Monuments exclusively
- Spawns inside and around the monument structure

Drops:
- Prismarine Shard (0-2)
- Prismarine Crystal (0-1)
- Raw Cod (0-1)
- Wet Sponge (Elder Guardian only — regular Guardian: rare)

Special Abilities:
- Laser attack: charges for 2 seconds showing a yellow-to-orange beam, then deals burst damage
- Thorns effect: deals 2 damage to any entity that hits it with a melee attack
- Eye on its body tracks the player during laser charge
- Pulses its spines while attacking
- Inflicts Mining Fatigue on nearby players (via Elder Guardian — but Guardians contribute to the monument defense)

Weaknesses:
- Breaking line-of-sight interrupts the laser charge
- Out of water (takes suffocation damage — no, actually they can survive briefly on land)
- Attack it during laser charge start to take no damage (laser must fully charge)
- Axe vs shield is effective due to Thorns

Immunities:
- Drowning

Behavior:
- Patrols the Ocean Monument area
- Fires laser at players who get too close
- Retracts spines when swimming, extends when targeting
- Swims away after firing then charges again

Sounds: Electrical humming, pulsing laser sounds

Notes:
- Ocean Monuments contain 8 Elder Guardians and many Guardians
- Prismarine materials are used for decorative building blocks
- Prismarine Crystals are used to craft Sea Lanterns (underwater light)
- Defeating Ocean Monuments is a major mid-game challenge
- Guardian farms are among the most efficient XP and fish farms in the game

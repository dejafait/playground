Name: Strider
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Nether

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.175 (land — slow) / 0.40 (on lava — fast)
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Nether (on Lava Sea surface)
- Basalt Deltas
- Crimson Forest (lava lakes)
- Spawns in groups, babies sometimes spawn on adult (like Chicken Jockeys)

Drops:
- String (2-5)

Taming and Riding:
- Feed Warped Fungus to tame attitude (becomes friendly)
- Place Saddle on tamed Strider to ride
- Steer with Warped Fungus on a Stick (like Carrot on a Stick for pigs)
- Speed boost when right-clicking with Warped Fungus on a Stick

Special Abilities:
- Walks on Lava — the ONLY rideable mob that can traverse lava safely
- Shakes and takes cold damage on land or in water (outside lava)
- Color changes from red/purple to shivering grey when out of lava (visual health indicator)
- Baby Striders sometimes spawn riding on adult Striders

Breeding:
- Feed 2 Striders Warped Fungus

Behavior:
- Walks on lava surface
- Moves slowly on land and trembles
- Follows players holding Warped Fungus
- Baby Striders ride adults

Sounds: Gurgling, clicking, humming

Notes:
- The only way to safely cross the massive Nether lava seas (pre-bridge/boat)
- Essential for Nether exploration before building bridges or using boats on lava
- Boats on Nether lava is an alternative strategy but faster with a Strider
- String drops make Striders a minor renewable String source in the Nether
- Visually unique — long legs standing above lava, purple/crimson coloring

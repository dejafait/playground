Name: Frog
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Amphibian

Health: 10 HP (5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None (eats small mobs with tongue — see Special Abilities)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.50 on land / swimmer
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Swamp
- Mangrove Swamp

Variants (3 types based on temperature where tadpole grows):
- Temperate / Orange Frog — Swamp, Mangrove Swamp
- Cold / Green Frog — Tundra, Taiga, Frozen biomes
- Warm / White Frog — Desert, Jungle, Savanna

Drops:
- Nothing directly

Special Abilities:
- Eats Small Slimes: Kills small slimes and drops Slimeball
- Eats Small Magma Cubes: Kills small magma cubes and drops a Froglight (color depends on frog variant):
  - Ochre Froglight (orange frog)
  - Verdant Froglight (green frog)
  - Pearlescent Froglight (white frog)
- Jumps very high relative to size
- Semi-aquatic — swims and walks on land
- Lays eggs (Frogspawn) in water when bred

Breeding:
- Feed 2 Frogs Slimeballs
- Pregnant frog lays Frogspawn (group of eggs) on water surface
- Frogspawn hatches into Tadpoles after a few minutes

Behavior:
- Jumps periodically
- Sits in water
- Eats small slimes/magma cubes with tongue
- Tadpoles grow into temperature-appropriate frog variant

Sounds: Croaking

Notes:
- The only way to obtain Froglight blocks (light sources) is via frogs eating magma cubes
- Building a frog farm in the Nether (small magma cubes + frogs = froglight farm)
- 3 unique Froglight colors require 3 frog variants — biome-specific collection
- Added in 1.19 (The Wild Update)
- One of the most charming newer mobs in the game

Name: Zombie
Type: Hostile
Category: Undead

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: 3 (Easy) / 4 (Normal) / 6 (Hard)
Armor: 2
Movement Speed: 0.23 (faster when pursuing player)
XP Dropped: 5 (adult) / 12 (baby)

Spawn Locations:
- Overworld (night, light level 0)
- Caves and dungeons
- Zombie spawners in dungeons

Drops:
- Rotten Flesh (0-2)
- Carrot (rare, 2.5%)
- Potato (rare, 2.5%)
- Iron Ingot (rare, 2.5%)
- Any armor/weapon if equipped (8.5%)

Special Abilities:
- Burns in direct sunlight (unless wearing helmet)
- Can call zombie reinforcements when attacked
- Can pick up and equip dropped items
- Breaks down wooden doors on Hard difficulty
- Baby zombies move faster and can ride chickens (Chicken Jockey)
- Zombies that drown transform into Drowned

Weaknesses:
- Sunlight (catches fire)
- Fire and lava
- Holy water / potions of Healing deal damage

Immunities:
- Poison
- Regeneration does not affect them

Behavior:
- Wanders aimlessly at night or in dark areas
- Chases players within 40 blocks (can be increased if player has been seen)
- Avoids sunlight during the day (seeks shade)
- Groups up when reinforcements are called

Sounds: Groaning, shuffling

Notes:
- One of the most common hostile mobs
- Baby zombies are smaller, faster, and worth more XP
- Zombies wearing armor won't burn in sunlight
- Can be found in zombie villages (zombie villagers)

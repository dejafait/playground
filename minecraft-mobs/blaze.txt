Name: Blaze
Type: Hostile
Category: Nether Monster

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: Fireball 5 + fire / Melee 6 (if player too close)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.23 (flies)
XP Dropped: 10

Spawn Locations:
- Nether Fortresses (natural spawning)
- Blaze Spawners in Nether Fortresses

Drops:
- Blaze Rod (0-1, doubled with Looting)

Special Abilities:
- Flies and hovers in the air
- Fires a rapid burst of 3 fireballs
- Charges up before firing (emits fire particles and spins)
- Sets targets on fire with fireballs
- Immune to fire and lava damage
- Takes damage from Snowballs (3 damage each)
- Takes damage from Water and Snow

Weaknesses:
- Snowballs (3 damage per hit — 7 snowballs to kill)
- Water and rain deal damage
- Cold or water-based attacks

Immunities:
- Fire
- Lava

Behavior:
- Hovers above the ground
- Rises and drops while pursuing player
- Charges up with fire particles before releasing 3-fireball burst
- Retreats slightly between bursts

Sounds: Breath sounds, fire crackling

Notes:
- Essential mob — Blaze Rods are required to reach The End (for Brewing Stands and Eyes of Ender)
- One of only 2 ways to obtain Blaze Powder
- Found only in Nether Fortresses, making Fortress-finding critical for progression
- Yellow-orange in color with floating rods orbiting its body
- Snowballs are the budget counter — cheap and effective

Name: Piglin
Type: Neutral (hostile without gold armor)
Category: Nether / Humanoid

Health: 16 HP (8 hearts)
Attack Damage: 5 (sword, Easy) / 8 (Normal) / 13 (Hard) / Crossbow varies
Armor: 2 (with armor)
Movement Speed: 0.35
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Crimson Forest (Nether)
- Bastion Remnants (common)

Drops:
- Gold Nuggets (0-1)
- Various barter items if killed while trading
- Crossbow (8.5%)
- Golden Sword (8.5%)
- Gold Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, Boots (various rarities)

Special Abilities:
- Barters: right-clicking with Gold Ingot initiates trade for random items
  (Barter loot: Ender Pearls, Leather, Obsidian, Potions, Arrows, etc.)
- Attacks players not wearing any gold armor
- Hunts and attacks Hoglins
- Hostile to Wither Skeletons and the Wither
- Retreats from Soul Fire, Soul Torches, and Zombified Piglins
- Picks up gold items from the ground

Barter Loot Includes:
- Ender Pearls, Fire Resistance Potions, Gravel, Leather, Nether Brick
- Iron Boots (enchanted), Iron Nuggets, Magma Cream, Obsidian, Splash Potion of Fire Resistance, String, Water Bottle

Weaknesses:
- Soul Fire and Soul-based light sources (they flee)
- Wearing gold armor makes them passive
- Zombify in Overworld / End (~15 seconds)

Immunities:
- Fire and lava

Behavior:
- Attacks players without gold armor on sight
- Investigates gold items dropped on the ground
- Hunts Hoglins in packs
- Celebratory behavior after a Hoglin kill
- Inspects gold ingot when given for bartering (5-second delay)

Sounds: Snorting pig-like sounds with aggressive grunts

Notes:
- Bartering is the main way to obtain Ender Pearls in the Nether
- Must wear at least one piece of gold armor to be safe near Piglins
- Piglins become Zombified Piglins in the Overworld or End
- Baby Piglins exist — they play and are never hostile
- Piglin Brutes are a separate, always-hostile variant found in Bastions

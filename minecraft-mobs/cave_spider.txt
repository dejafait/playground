Name: Cave Spider
Type: Hostile
Category: Arthropod

Health: 12 HP (6 hearts)
Attack Damage: 2 (Easy, no poison) / 2 + Poison 7s (Normal) / 3 + Poison 15s (Hard)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.30
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Mineshafts (spawned exclusively from Cave Spider spawners)
- Only found via spawners — do not spawn naturally in the world

Drops:
- String (0-2)
- Spider Eye (1/3 chance)

Special Abilities:
- Venomous bite — inflicts Poison on Normal and Hard difficulty
- Smaller than regular spider (0.7 blocks tall) — fits through 1-block gaps
- Can climb walls just like regular spiders
- Fits through gaps that regular spiders cannot

Weaknesses:
- Bane of Arthropods enchantment (extra damage + slowness)
- Milk cures poison effect
- Poison doesn't kill (stops at 1 HP)

Immunities:
- Poison effect (the status effect doesn't harm them)

Behavior:
- Always hostile (unlike regular spiders, doesn't turn neutral in light)
- Pursues players aggressively
- Climbs through tight spaces in mineshafts
- Swarms in packs near spawners

Sounds: Same as regular spider — hissing, skittering

Notes:
- Blue-tinted color differentiates it from regular spiders
- Most dangerous in mineshafts where they mob the player in tight corridors
- Spawners found in abandoned mineshafts surrounded by cobwebs
- Smallest hostile mob that can poison the player

Name: Cow
Type: Passive
Category: Animal

Health: 10 HP (5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.20
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Plains
- Forest
- Birch Forest
- Dark Forest
- Meadow
- Most grassy overworld biomes

Drops:
- Raw Beef (1-3, Cooked Beef if on fire)
- Leather (0-2)

Breeding:
- Feed 2 Cows Wheat to breed
- Baby calf grows into adult after ~20 minutes
- 5 minute cooldown between breeding attempts

Special Abilities:
- Can be milked with an empty Bucket (right-click)
- Milk removes all status effects from the player (curative item)
- Source of both food (beef) and leather (armor crafting)
- Mooshroom is a mushroom-covered variant found only in Mushroom Islands

Behavior:
- Wanders peacefully
- Moves away when hit but doesn't flee far
- Follows players holding Wheat
- Babies follow adults

Sounds: Mooing

Notes:
- One of the most essential survival mobs — leather for books/armor, beef for food
- Milk is uniquely useful: cures every status effect instantly (vital against Witches, Wither effect, etc.)
- One of the earliest mobs in Minecraft (Beta era)
- Cow farms are among the most common automated farms
- Required for Leather, which is needed for Books → Bookshelves → Enchanting Table setup

Name: Turtle
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Aquatic

Health: 30 HP (15 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.25 (water) / 0.08 (land — very slow)
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Warm Beaches
- Lukewarm Ocean Beaches
- Stony Shores (rarely)

Drops:
- Seagrass (0-2 when killed as adult)
- Scute (1, when baby turtle grows into adult — not on death)

Breeding:
- Feed 2 Turtles Seagrass
- Pregnant turtle (slightly larger) will attempt to return to its HOME beach (birth beach)
- Lays 1-4 Turtle Eggs on home beach (can only lay on Sand)
- Eggs hatch over time into Baby Turtles → grow into adults

Turtle Shell Helmet:
- Crafted from 5 Scutes
- Provides Water Breathing +10 seconds when underwater
- Functions like a helmet with 2 armor points

Special Mechanics:
- Each turtle has a "home beach" — it always returns to the same beach to lay eggs
- Turtle Eggs are fragile: Zombies, Husks, Drowned, Skeletons, and Foxes intentionally stomp on them
- Eggs take 1-3 Minecraft nights to hatch (each night cracks the egg 1 stage)
- Baby turtles are the smallest mob in the game

Behavior:
- Wanders beach and ocean
- Swims fast in water
- Very slow on land
- Pregnant turtle pathfinds back to birth beach regardless of distance

Sounds: Bubbling sounds, soft movement sounds

Notes:
- Scutes from baby turtle growth are the primary reward (not from killing)
- Turtle Eggs are a key mechanic — protecting them from zombies at night is a challenge
- The Turtle Shell potion ingredient (Scute) is used for Potion of the Turtle Master
- Baby turtles are tiny — only 0.12 blocks tall
- One of the most unique progression mechanics (protecting eggs through hatching)

Name: Ocelot
Type: Passive
Category: Animal

Health: 10 HP (5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None (doesn't attack)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.30
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Jungle
- Bamboo Jungle
- Sparse Jungle

Drops:
- Nothing

Trust Mechanic (not taming):
- Feed Raw Cod or Raw Salmon while approaching very slowly
- Earns "trust" but ocelot remains wild and does not follow the player
- Trusted ocelots stay near the player area and don't flee

Special Abilities:
- Scares Creepers — Creepers flee from ocelots within 6 blocks
- Scares Phantoms — Phantoms avoid swooping near ocelots
- Very fast — one of the fastest land animals
- Cannot be tamed into a companion (unlike before 1.14)
- Cannot be given commands (sit/follow)

Behavior:
- Extremely skittish — runs away when player approaches
- Crouching and approaching slowly lets you feed it
- Hunts Chickens
- Sprints away if player moves too fast

Sounds: Meowing (similar to cats), purring

Notes:
- Before Java 1.14, ocelots could be tamed into cats — this was split into two separate mobs
- Cats are now distinct from Ocelots — Cats spawn in villages; Ocelots spawn in jungles
- Despite not being tameable, they're still useful anti-Creeper deterrents in jungle bases
- One of the rarer mobs to find in survival due to sparse jungle spawning
- Very fast movement speed makes them hard to catch

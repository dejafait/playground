Name: Trader Llama
Type: Neutral (spits when provoked)
Category: Animal / Pack Animal

Health: 15-30 HP (varies)
Attack Damage: Spit — 1 damage
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.175
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Always spawned with Wandering Traders (2 per trader)
- Does not spawn independently

Drops:
- Leather (0-2)

Equipment:
- Always wears a Blue or Cyan Carpet (distinguishes from wild Llama)
- Can carry a Chest (like regular Llama — but chest is for Wandering Trader's use)

Special Abilities:
- Same as regular Llama:
  - Can carry Chest (inventory slots based on Strength)
  - Spits at attackers (1 damage)
  - Forms caravan when leashed
- When Wandering Trader is killed, Trader Llamas remain in the world
- Can be tamed after the Wandering Trader despawns/dies

Differences from Regular Llama:
- Wears blue/cyan carpet by default (visual indicator)
- Spawns exclusively with Wandering Traders
- Stays near the Wandering Trader while it lives
- Same stats and behavior otherwise

Behavior:
- Follows Wandering Trader in a caravan
- Spits at entities that attack the Wandering Trader
- After Trader death, behaves like a normal Llama

Sounds: Humming, spitting

Notes:
- The Blue Carpet differentiates them visually from wild Llamas
- After the Wandering Trader despawns, Trader Llamas are often abandoned
- Killing the Wandering Trader to get its two Trader Llamas is a common strategy for free Llamas
- Killing a Trader Llama gives Hero of the Village status a small reduction — be careful in villages
- Functionally identical to regular Llamas once the Wandering Trader is gone

Name: Skeleton Horse
Type: Passive (after taming) / Trap (before triggered)
Category: Undead / Animal / Rideable

Health: 15 HP
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.20
Jump Height: 0.4-1.0
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Skeleton Trap: Spawned by lightning strike in a thunderstorm (rare)
  (A lone horse stands in the rain — approaching within 10 blocks triggers the trap)
  → 4 Skeleton Horsemen appear (Skeleton + Skeleton Horse, each Skeleton wears enchanted helmet)
- Via spawn egg / commands

Drops:
- Bones (0-2)

Taming:
- Kill the Skeleton rider — the horse becomes rideable immediately
- No taming process needed — just ride it
- Does not require a saddle in some versions

Special Abilities:
- Can breathe underwater indefinitely
- Can be ridden underwater without sinking
- Wears a saddle permanently (cannot be removed in Java)
- Does not need to eat — no hunger mechanic
- Burns in sunlight... actually does NOT burn (undead horse but immune to sun)
- Skeleton riders wear enchanted helmets (prevents burning — by extension the horse roams safely)

Immunities:
- Drowning

Behavior:
- The trap horse stands still in rain
- Approaching triggers the explosion of 4 skeletons on skeleton horses
- After riders killed, the horses are docile

Sounds: Same as regular horse, slightly more hollow

Notes:
- The Skeleton Trap is one of Minecraft's most memorable surprise moments
- Best underwater horse — can swim at horse speed underwater
- The "zombie horse" exists but does not spawn naturally in survival
- Each Skeleton rider wears an enchanted iron helmet and carries an enchanted bow
- Good late-game mount due to water-breathing capability (useful for ocean exploration)

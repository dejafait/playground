Name: Piglin Brute
Type: Hostile (always — no gold armor exception)
Category: Nether / Humanoid

Health: 50 HP (25 hearts)
Attack Damage: 13 (Easy) / 19 (Normal) / 28 (Hard)
Armor: 2
Movement Speed: 0.35
XP Dropped: 20

Spawn Locations:
- Bastion Remnants only (guards treasure rooms and chests)
- Does not respawn after being killed

Drops:
- Golden Axe (100%, may be enchanted)

Special Abilities:
- Always hostile — wearing gold armor has NO effect
- Wields a golden axe
- Higher HP and damage than regular Piglins
- Zombifies in the Overworld/End like regular Piglins
- Does not barter — purely a guard mob

Weaknesses:
- Standard combat (just tough)
- Smite enchantment does not work (not undead while alive)

Immunities:
- Fire and lava

Behavior:
- Attacks the player on sight regardless of gold armor
- Aggressive and single-minded
- Guards the loot rooms of Bastions

Sounds: Angry snorting and grunting

Notes:
- One of the toughest non-boss Nether mobs
- Specifically added to make Bastion Remnant exploration risky
- Does not spawn naturally elsewhere — only in Bastions
- The golden axe can be enchanted and is a decent early-game weapon
- Once the Brutes in a Bastion are killed, they never respawn

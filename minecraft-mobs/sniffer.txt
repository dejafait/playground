Name: Sniffer
Type: Passive
Category: Ancient Animal

Health: 14 HP (7 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.10 (very slow)
XP Dropped: 1-3
Size: Largest passive mob in the game

Spawn Locations:
- Does not spawn naturally — must be hatched from a Sniffer Egg
- Sniffer Eggs found in Suspicious Sand/Gravel at Warm Ocean Ruins (using Brush tool)

Drops:
- Nothing

Sniffing Mechanic:
- Sniffers sniff the air, then dig in Dirt, Grass, Podzol, Moss, Mud, or Muddy Mangrove Roots
- Digging uncovers Ancient Seeds:
  - Torchflower Seeds
  - Pitcher Pod
- Sniffs every 8 minutes approximately (one seed per sniff)
- Seeds grow into unique decorative plants not obtainable any other way

Ancient Plants Grown from Sniffer Seeds:
- Torchflower: Bright orange flower, used in breeding Sniffers and as decoration
- Pitcher Plant: Tall decorative pitcher-shaped plant (2 blocks tall)

Breeding:
- Feed 2 adult Sniffers Torchflower Seeds
- Baby Sniffer grows into adult (~20 minutes)

Behavior:
- Wanders slowly
- Periodically stops and sniffs ground
- Digs with front legs to uncover seeds
- Baby Sniffers also sniff but don't uncover seeds

Sounds: Deep sniffing, rumbling

Notes:
- Won the Minecraft Live 2022 Mob Vote
- Added in 1.20 (Trails and Tales update)
- Lore: Ancient mob that went "extinct" — eggs preserved in ocean ruins
- The only way to obtain Torchflowers and Pitcher Plants
- Largest mob in the game (excluding bosses) — massive and impressive-looking
- Torchflowers and Pitcher Pods are used in decoration and as Sniffer breeding items

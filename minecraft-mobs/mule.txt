Name: Mule
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Rideable

Health: 15-30 HP (varies)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0 (cannot wear horse armor)
Movement Speed: 0.175-0.225 (between horse and donkey speed)
Jump Height: 0.5
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Does not spawn naturally — bred from a Horse + Donkey
- Only obtainable through breeding

Drops:
- Leather (0-2)

Taming:
- Born tame if bred from two tamed parents
- Can still be ridden right away after breeding

Equipment:
- Saddle (required for riding)
- Chest (attach with chest in hand — gives 15 inventory slots)

Special Abilities:
- Combines moderate speed (between horse and donkey)
- Can carry a Chest like a Donkey
- Sterile — cannot be bred further
- Can have better stats than a Donkey due to Horse genetic inheritance

Behavior:
- Same as Donkey generally

Sounds: Combination of Horse and Donkey sounds

Notes:
- Real mules are also sterile hybrids — accurately reflected in the game
- Better transport option than Donkey alone due to inherited Horse speed genes
- The go-to storage mount for mid-game players
- If you breed a fast Horse with a Donkey, the Mule will be noticeably faster than a plain Donkey
- Max stats are: 15 HP + Donkey speed = still not as fast as best horses

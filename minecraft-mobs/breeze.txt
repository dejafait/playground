Name: Breeze
Type: Hostile
Category: Monster / Trial Chambers

Health: 30 HP (15 hearts)
Attack Damage: Wind Charge impact 1 + knockback (direct) / Environmental hazards from activation
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Jumps and dashes (~0.60)
XP Dropped: 10

Spawn Locations:
- Trial Chambers exclusively (generated underground structures)
- Spawned from Trial Spawners in combat rooms

Drops:
- Breeze Rod (1-2)

Special Abilities:
- Fires Wind Charges — projectiles that create a burst of wind on impact
- Wind bursts push players and mobs (knockback)
- Wind bursts activate levers, buttons, trapdoors, and bells within range
  (Can trigger traps in the Trial Chamber against the player)
- Immune to almost all projectiles — arrows, tridents, snowballs all deflected
- Jumps to high ledges to maintain range advantage
- Very mobile — constantly repositioning

Weaknesses:
- Melee attacks (can't deflect player sword)
- Area-of-effect splash potions

Immunities:
- All projectiles (arrows, snowballs, tridents deflected back)
- Fall damage

Behavior:
- Leaps around the arena constantly
- Fires Wind Charges from range
- Activates environmental hazards intentionally
- Uses verticality to stay out of melee range

Sounds: Whooshing wind sounds, energetic movement

Notes:
- Added in 1.21 (Tricky Trials update)
- Breeze Rods are used to craft Wind Charges (throwable item) and Mace (new weapon)
- The environmental activation mechanic makes it uniquely dangerous in Trial Chambers
- Represents a new "arena fighter" mob that uses the environment as a weapon
- The Mace crafted from Breeze Rods deals bonus damage based on fall height

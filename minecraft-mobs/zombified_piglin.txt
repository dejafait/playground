Name: Zombified Piglin
Type: Neutral (hostile when provoked)
Category: Undead / Nether

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: 9 (melee, Easy) / 13 (Normal) / 19 (Hard)
Armor: 2
Movement Speed: 0.23 (0.32 aggro)
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Nether (all biomes)
- Overworld / End — when a Pig is struck by lightning
- Near Nether Portals in the Overworld
- Nether Portal in the Overworld (occasionally walk through)
- When a Piglin enters the Overworld or End (transforms)

Drops:
- Rotten Flesh (0-1)
- Gold Nugget (0-1)
- Gold Ingot (rare, 2.5%)
- Golden Sword (rare, 8.5%)
- Golden Helmet (rare, 8.5%)

Special Abilities:
- Passive unless attacked — one hit causes entire group to aggro
- Group aggro: nearby Zombified Piglins within 67 blocks join the attack
- Aggro lasts 20-39.95 seconds
- Immune to fire and lava
- Anger timer — eventually forgets and returns to neutral

Weaknesses:
- Smite enchantment (undead — takes bonus damage)
- Standard weapons (after angering them)

Immunities:
- Fire and lava

Behavior:
- Wanders aimlessly when neutral
- Becomes very aggressive when any member of their group is attacked
- Does not forget aggro quickly — pursues for a long time
- Holds golden sword

Sounds: Gurgling zombie sounds mixed with pig oinks

Notes:
- Formerly called "Zombie Pigman" before 1.16
- Safe to farm for gold but extreme care needed not to hit one
- One of the best gold farms in the game
- Will also become aggressive if a player hits one accidentally (e.g., splash potion area)
- Piglins that enter the Overworld or End zombify after ~15 seconds

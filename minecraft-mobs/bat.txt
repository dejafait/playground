Name: Bat
Type: Passive
Category: Animal

Health: 6 HP (3 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Flies at ~0.30
XP Dropped: 0

Spawn Locations:
- Caves (below Y=63 — spawns at light level 4 or below)
- Underground caverns
- Requires at least a 2×3 space to spawn
- During Halloween (Oct 20–Nov 3) they can spawn at slightly higher altitudes

Drops:
- Nothing

Special Abilities:
- Hangs upside down on ceilings when not flying
- Squeaks — can alert player to nearby cave areas
- Very agile flight — hard to hit
- Cannot be hurt by conventional fall damage (flies always)

Behavior:
- Flies randomly in caves
- Hangs from ceilings when resting
- Startles and flies away when player approaches
- Does not attack, drop items, or provide any game mechanic benefit

Sounds: High-pitched squeaking/chirping

Notes:
- One of the few mobs that provides zero gameplay benefit (no drops, no XP)
- Primarily atmospheric — indicates you're deep underground
- The squeak sounds are charming but serve no functional purpose
- Sometimes flies into lava and dies, creating a sad sound effect
- Prior to 1.20.3, bats were fairly useless; discussions of "improving" bats have been ongoing in the community

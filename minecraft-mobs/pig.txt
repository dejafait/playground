Name: Pig
Type: Passive
Category: Animal

Health: 10 HP (5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.25
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Plains
- Forest
- Most grassy biomes (common)

Drops:
- Raw Porkchop (1-3, Cooked Porkchop if on fire)

Breeding:
- Feed 2 Pigs Carrots, Potatoes, or Beetroot

Special Abilities:
- Can be saddled and ridden (right-click with Saddle)
- Steering requires Carrot on a Stick (crafted from Stick + Carrot)
- Boost speed by right-clicking with Carrot on a Stick (durability cost)
- Struck by lightning → transforms into a Zombified Piglin
- Baby pigs exist (smaller, follow adults)

Behavior:
- Wanders peacefully
- Follows players holding Carrots, Potatoes, or Beetroot
- If ridden off a cliff, takes fall damage normally (rider should use Feather Falling or jump carefully)

Sounds: Oinking, squealing when hurt

Notes:
- One of the original Minecraft mobs — the Creeper was a failed pig model
- Riding a pig is largely a novelty — horses are significantly faster and better
- Pigs are the only non-mob-of-your-dimension thing that Minecraft's lightning specifically transforms
- Porkchop is a very good food item (high saturation)
- Pig farms are a common food source in early-to-mid game

Name: Skeleton
Type: Hostile
Category: Undead

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: Arrow damage varies by difficulty and distance (1-5 per arrow)
Armor: 2
Movement Speed: 0.25
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Overworld (night, light level 0)
- Caves and dungeons
- Skeleton spawners in dungeons
- Nether Fortresses (rarely, replaced by Wither Skeletons)

Drops:
- Bones (0-2)
- Arrows (0-2)
- Bow (8.5% chance, may be enchanted)
- Any equipped items (rare)

Special Abilities:
- Shoots arrows at players from a distance
- Strafe sideways while shooting to avoid hits
- Burns in direct sunlight (unless wearing helmet)
- Accuracy increases on Hard difficulty
- Can pick up and use items on the ground
- Will seek shade or water to avoid sunlight
- Knocks back player with arrows

Weaknesses:
- Sunlight (catches fire)
- Fire and lava
- Melee combat at close range (tries to back away)

Immunities:
- Poison
- Drowning (technically but can still die in water indirectly)

Behavior:
- Keeps distance from player and shoots arrows
- Circles player to strafe
- Flees into shade at sunrise
- Will switch to melee if player gets very close

Sounds: Rattling bones, bow twang

Notes:
- One of the original Minecraft mobs
- Skeletons killed by creeper explosions drop music discs
- Can spawn riding spiders (Spider Jockey)
- Wears Jack o'Lantern during Halloween event

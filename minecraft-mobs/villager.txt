Name: Villager
Type: Passive
Category: Humanoid / NPC

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: None (does not attack)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.50
XP Dropped: 0 (small XP from trades)

Spawn Locations:
- Villages (all biome variants)
- Igloo basements (1 Zombie Villager, 1 regular Villager)
- Bred by player action (2 villagers + beds + food)

Drops:
- Nothing

Professions (determined by job site block):
- Armorer (Blast Furnace) — sells armor
- Butcher (Smoker) — sells meat/food
- Cartographer (Cartography Table) — sells maps
- Cleric (Brewing Stand) — sells potions/ender pearls
- Farmer (Composter) — buys/sells crops and food
- Fisherman (Barrel) — sells fish/fishing rods
- Fletcher (Fletching Table) — sells bows/arrows/crossbows
- Leatherworker (Cauldron) — sells leather armor
- Librarian (Lectern) — sells enchanted books
- Mason / Stone Mason (Stonecutter) — sells stone materials
- Shepherd (Loom) — sells wool/dye/tapestries
- Toolsmith (Smithing Table) — sells tools
- Weaponsmith (Grindstone) — sells swords/axes
- Nitwit (no job site) — cannot trade, useless for trading
- Unemployed — can gain a profession if near a job site block

Special Mechanics:
- Trading levels up (Novice → Apprentice → Journeyman → Expert → Master)
- Leveling unlocks better trades
- Curing a Zombie Villager gives massive permanent discounts on that villager's trades
- Villagers gossip — treatment (killing, curing, trading) affects reputation with other villagers
- Iron Golems spawn to protect villages (1 per 10 villagers)
- Sleep in beds at night — villages without beds become unprotected
- Breed when they have enough beds and food (any thrown crops)
- Flee from hostile mobs and ring bells to alert others
- Get scared by zombies, pillagers, ravagers, etc.

Weaknesses:
- No combat ability — relies entirely on Iron Golems and players for protection
- Zombies can turn them into Zombie Villagers
- Lightning within 4 blocks converts them into Witches

Behavior:
- Wanders village during day
- Trades with players (right-click)
- Sleeps at night (path to nearest unclaimed bed)
- Socializes with other villagers (faces and "hmm" sounds)
- Farmers plant/harvest crops
- Children exist (baby villagers who play)

Sounds: "Hmm" and "Huh" and trading sounds, bell ringing

Notes:
- Librarian villagers with Mending enchantment books are among the most sought trades
- Cured Zombie Villagers offer 1-emerald trades for valuable items
- A trading hall with multiple cured villagers is one of the most powerful player setups
- Villagers form the backbone of Minecraft's economy system
- Village biome determines villager appearance (Plains, Desert, Savanna, Snowy, Jungle, Swamp, Taiga styles)

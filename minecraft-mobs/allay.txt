Name: Allay
Type: Passive
Category: Utility / Mob

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Flies at 0.50-0.60
XP Dropped: 0

Spawn Locations:
- Pillager Outpost cages (2-3 in wooden cages around the outpost)
- Woodland Mansion jail cells (within specific rooms)

Drops:
- Nothing

Special Abilities:
- Item Collector: Give an Allay any item → it will search the surrounding area for matching items
  and bring them back to you (or to a Note Block it bonded with)
- Note Block Bonding: Play a Note Block near an Allay — it bonds to it and will deliver collected
  items to that Note Block location when it plays
- Duplication: When a Note Block plays and the Allay is holding items and dancing,
  feeding it an Amethyst Shard duplicates it (creates a second Allay — 5 minute cooldown)
- Dances when music plays from a Jukebox or Note Block

Item Delivery Range:
- Searches a large radius for matching items
- Returns to player or bonded Note Block with collected items

Behavior:
- Follows the player who gave it an item
- Flies around collecting items of the matching type
- Dances when music is playing nearby
- Cannot attack — completely passive

Sounds: Gentle humming, happy musical tones

Notes:
- Won the Minecraft Live 2021 Mob Vote
- Added in 1.19 (The Wild Update)
- The Note Block bonding mechanic makes Allays perfect for automated item sorting systems
- Duplicating Allays is the only way to get more beyond what spawn in structures
- One of the most useful utility mobs for automation-focused players
- The dancing behavior is purely cosmetic but endearing

Name: Warden
Type: Hostile
Category: Monster / Deep Dark

Health: 500 HP (250 hearts)
Attack Damage: Melee 16 (Easy) / 30 (Normal) / 45 (Hard)
              Sonic Shriek 6 (Easy) / 10 (Normal) / 13 (Hard) — ignores armor
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.30
XP Dropped: 5 (intentionally low — designed to be avoided, not farmed)

Spawn Locations:
- Deep Dark biome only (deepest underground levels)
- Summoned when Sculk Shriekers are triggered 3 times

Drops:
- Sculk Catalyst (rarely)
- 5 XP

Special Abilities:
- Completely blind — detects players via:
  1. Vibrations (walking, breaking blocks, projectiles, opening chests)
  2. Smell — detects player proximity regardless of movement after 9 seconds
- Sonic Boom attack: ranged attack that completely IGNORES all armor and shields
- Inflicts Darkness effect (vision pulses black)
- Emerges from the ground dramatically when summoned
- Anger system: tracks multiple entities and targets the angriest one
- Not aggressive until it detects something — avoidable

Weaknesses:
- Can be distracted with snowballs/arrows thrown elsewhere
- Sneaking reduces vibration
- Wool blocks dampen vibrations
- Can be "snuck past" with enough care

Immunities:
- Does not take extra damage from any enchantment type
- All armor is ignored by Sonic Boom

Behavior:
- Emerges from ground when Sculk Shrieker activates 3 times
- Investigates vibrations (footsteps, projectiles, blocks broken)
- Sniffs the air — detects players by scent after cooldown
- Submerges back into ground if no threats detected for 60 seconds
- Retreats and de-spawns when calm for long enough

Sounds: Deep heartbeat-like thuds, bone-cracking steps, eerie shriek

Notes:
- The strongest overworld mob — intended to be avoided not killed
- 500 HP and 30 damage (Normal) makes it almost impossible to outlast
- Deep Dark contains Ancient City structures with powerful loot
- Swift Sneak enchanted leggings (found in Ancient Cities) are designed for Warden avoidance
- The Warden represents a new "stealth challenge" gameplay loop in Minecraft
- Even with full Netherite armor, the Sonic Boom will kill a player in a few hits

Name: Vex
Type: Hostile
Category: Illager / Spirit

Health: 14 HP (7 hearts)
Attack Damage: 9 (Easy) / 13 (Normal) / 17 (Hard)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Flies at 0.60
XP Dropped: 3

Spawn Locations:
- Summoned exclusively by Evokers (3 at a time)
- Does not spawn naturally

Drops:
- Iron Sword (rare)

Special Abilities:
- Can fly through any solid block — walls, floors, ceilings offer no protection
- High damage for its size
- Glows red when charging toward a player
- Has a short lifespan — after 30-119 seconds (random) it begins taking 1 HP/second of damage
  until it dies (unless re-summoned by another Evoker)
- Cannot be leashed or bred

Weaknesses:
- Low HP — easy to kill if you can hit it
- Short natural lifespan (eventually kills itself)

Immunities:
- Terrain/blocks (phases through them)

Behavior:
- Charges at players aggressively
- Passes through walls and floors to reach the target
- Glows red while in attack mode
- Fades over time unless supported by more Evoker summons

Sounds: Ghostly whimpering, high-pitched shrieking

Notes:
- One of the most terrifying mobs due to phasing through walls
- Makes hiding in rooms impossible when Evokers are present
- Small glowing figure — inspired by ghost/imp designs
- The lifespan mechanic means Vexes are temporary — don't last forever even if not killed
- Main danger is during Woodland Mansion raids where Evokers summon them repeatedly

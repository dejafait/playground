Name: Slime
Type: Hostile
Category: Monster

Health: Large 16 HP / Medium 4 HP / Small 1 HP
Attack Damage: Large 4 / Medium 2 / Small 0 (contact only)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Large 0.15 / Medium 0.30 / Small 0.45 (smaller = faster)
XP Dropped: Large 4 / Medium 2 / Small 1

Spawn Locations:
- Swamp biomes (surface, night, Y=50-70)
- Slime chunks (underground, below Y=40, any light level)
- Superflat worlds (common)

Drops:
- Slimeball (0-2, only from Small Slimes)

Special Abilities:
- Splits into 2-4 smaller Slimes when killed (Large → Medium, Medium → Small)
- Only Small Slimes drop Slimeballs
- Jumps toward player to attack
- Can see through walls (tracks player through terrain)
- Small Slimes deal no damage but are annoying and push player

Weaknesses:
- Fire
- Standard combat

Immunities:
- Fall damage

Behavior:
- Hops toward player
- Splits into smaller versions when killed
- Spawns in specific underground chunks (Slime Chunks)
- Surface spawning only in Swamps and only at certain moon phases

Sounds: Squelching, squishing

Notes:
- Slimeballs are used for leads, sticky pistons, slime blocks, and magma cream
- Slime chunks are fixed and can be found with seeds
- Moon phase affects slime spawning rates in swamps (full moon = most spawns)
- Essential farm mob for Redstone contraptions (slime blocks, sticky pistons)
- Tiny slimes can be kept as "pets" as they do no damage

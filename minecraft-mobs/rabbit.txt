Name: Rabbit
Type: Passive (Killer Bunny variant is Hostile)
Category: Animal

Health: 3 HP (1.5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None (Killer Bunny: 8 Normal / 5 Easy)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.30 (hops quickly)
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Desert
- Flower Forest
- Taiga
- Snowy Tundra
- Meadow
- Frozen Ocean

Color Variants (based on biome):
- Brown (common)
- White (snowy biomes)
- Black and White (forest)
- Gold (desert)
- Salt and Pepper (taiga)
- Black (rare)
- Toast (named variant — tribute to a developer's lost real rabbit)

Drops:
- Raw Rabbit (0-1)
- Rabbit Hide (0-1)
- Rabbit's Foot (10% chance — increased by Looting)

Special Items:
- Rabbit Hide: 4 Rabbit Hides → 1 Leather (useful early game in snowy biomes)
- Rabbit's Foot: Brewing ingredient for Potion of Leaping
- Raw Rabbit: Food (can be cooked or made into Rabbit Stew)

Special Variants:
- Killer Bunny: Only via commands / old Easter egg in Java — hostile, attacks players
  (reference to Monty Python and the Holy Grail "The Killer Rabbit of Caerbannog")
- Toast: Name tag a rabbit "Toast" — turns it into a black and white rabbit
  (tribute to a Reddit user's missing rabbit)

Behavior:
- Hops around quickly
- Very fast and hard to catch
- Follows players holding Carrots or Dandelions
- Flees when attacked

Sounds: Squeaking, soft hop sounds

Notes:
- Rabbit Stew is one of the highest-saturation food items in the game
- Rabbit farms are uncommon but the Rabbit's Foot makes them valuable
- The "Toast" variant is one of Minecraft's most emotional easter eggs
- Rabbits are the only mob that naturally flee from players
- The Killer Bunny can be summoned with: /summon rabbit ~ ~ ~ {RabbitType:99}

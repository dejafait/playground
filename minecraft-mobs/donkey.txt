Name: Donkey
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Rideable

Health: 15-30 HP (varies like horse)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0 (cannot wear horse armor)
Movement Speed: 0.175 (slower than horses)
Jump Height: 0.5 (cannot jump like horses)
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Plains
- Savanna

Drops:
- Leather (0-2)

Taming:
- Same as Horse — right-click repeatedly until tamed
- Feed food to speed up taming

Equipment:
- Saddle (required for riding/steering)
- Chest (attach with right-click while holding chest on tamed donkey)
  → Chest gives 15 inventory slots

Special Abilities:
- Can carry a Chest (15 inventory slots) — unlike Horses
- Can be bred with a Horse to produce a Mule
- Good choice for storage transport

Behavior:
- Same as Horse generally
- Brays occasionally

Sounds: Braying (hee-haw), snorting

Notes:
- Slower and lower-jumping than horses, but the chest storage is unique
- The only two chestable rideable mobs in the Overworld (Donkey and Mule)
- Cannot be used to breed with another Donkey to improve stats
- Breeding a Donkey with a Horse produces a Mule (which is sterile)
- The chest capacity varies — Donkeys have 15 slots vs Llamas which have 3-15 based on strength

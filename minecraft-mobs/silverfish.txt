Name: Silverfish
Type: Hostile
Category: Arthropod

Health: 8 HP (4 hearts)
Attack Damage: 1
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.30
Size: Very small (0.4 × 0.3 × 0.4)
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Strongholds (hidden in Monster Egg blocks — infested stone/brick)
- Extreme Hills / Windswept Hills biome surface (infested stone)
- Igloo basements (infested stone blocks)
- Silverfish spawner in Stronghold portal room

Drops:
- Nothing

Special Abilities:
- Hidden inside Monster Egg blocks (infested stone, cobblestone, stone bricks, etc.)
  — these blocks take slightly longer to mine and release a Silverfish when broken
- When a Silverfish is attacked, nearby Silverfish hiding in infested blocks will emerge
- Can re-enter nearby stone/brick blocks to hide
- Swarms the player when disturbed in groups
- Very fast movement for their size

Weaknesses:
- Bane of Arthropods enchantment
- Area-of-effect attacks kill groups
- Mining infested blocks with Silk Touch prevents spawning

Immunities:
- None

Behavior:
- Hidden until blocks are disturbed or they're triggered by combat
- Swarms and overwhelms player in tight Stronghold corridors
- Re-hides in blocks when idle

Sounds: Rustling, insect-like chittering

Notes:
- Infested blocks appear identical to normal stone — surprise reveal when mined
- Mining infested blocks with a sword kills the Silverfish before it can alert others
- Locating infested blocks (slightly slower to break) helps identify Stronghold proximity
- The End Portal is located in the Stronghold, making dealing with Silverfish unavoidable
- Bane of Arthropods III+ adds slowness and bonus damage — effective crowd control

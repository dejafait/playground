Name: Enderman
Type: Neutral (becomes hostile when looked at)
Category: Monster

Health: 40 HP (20 hearts)
Attack Damage: 7 (Easy) / 10 (Normal) / 15 (Hard)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.30 (normal) / 0.65 (aggro)
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Overworld (night, all biomes, rare)
- The Nether (all areas)
- The End (common — large groups on main island)

Drops:
- Ender Pearl (0-1)

Special Abilities:
- Teleports when attacked or hit by projectiles
- Teleports away from water and rain
- Becomes hostile when player looks directly at its head/body
- Can pick up and carry certain blocks (grass, dirt, sand, gravel, etc.)
- Places held blocks elsewhere in the world
- Teleports randomly when on fire
- Extremely long reach for melee attacks

Weaknesses:
- Water and rain deal continuous damage
- Looking at them without aggressing (wear pumpkin on head to look safely)
- Being in water forces teleportation and chip damage

Immunities:
- Projectiles (teleports away from arrows, snowballs, etc.)
- Does not burn in sunlight

Behavior:
- Neutral until player makes eye contact with its body
- Shakes and screams when aggroed
- Teleports around player in combat
- Picks up random blocks while wandering
- Teleports to dry land if it enters water

Sounds: Long, eerie moaning sounds, distorted speech

Notes:
- Name and behavior are inspired by the Slender Man creepypasta
- Makes unique spooky sounds that are reversed and pitch-shifted speech
- Wearing a pumpkin helmet prevents aggro from eye contact
- Essential to farm for Ender Pearls needed to reach The End

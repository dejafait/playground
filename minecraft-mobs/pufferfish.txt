Name: Pufferfish
Type: Passive (defends when approached)
Category: Animal / Aquatic

Health: 3 HP (1.5 hearts)
Attack Damage: 7 (fully puffed, poison + damage to nearby entities)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Swims slowly
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Warm Ocean
- Lukewarm Ocean

Drops:
- Pufferfish (1)
- Bone Meal (rarely)

Puffing Mechanic (3 states):
- Deflated (no threat): Normal small fish appearance
- Semi-puffed (nearby entity): Medium spiky appearance
- Fully puffed (entity within 3 blocks): Large spiky ball — deals Poison II to touching entities
  Also inflicts Nausea II for 7 seconds

Special Abilities:
- Puffs up when players or mobs approach within ~3 blocks
- Fully puffed Pufferfish poisons and damages all touching entities
- Only the fully puffed state deals damage
- Pufferfish item (when eaten): Inflicts player with Hunger III, Poison IV, Nausea II for ~15 seconds

Pufferfish Item Uses:
- Potion of Water Breathing (Brewing ingredient — Pufferfish + Awkward Potion)
- DO NOT eat raw — extremely toxic to player

Behavior:
- Swims slowly in warm ocean
- Automatically puffs up when threatened
- Puffs back down after threat moves away

Sounds: Blowing up sounds, squirting water

Notes:
- One of the only mobs that deals poison on contact without needing to attack
- The Water Breathing Potion is critical for ocean monument raiding and underwater builds
- Fishing can produce Pufferfish regardless of biome (treasure loot from fishing)
- Eating a Pufferfish intentionally is one of the worst food decisions in the game
- Only 3 HP — dies to almost anything

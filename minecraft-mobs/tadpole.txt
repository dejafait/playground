Name: Tadpole
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Amphibian (Baby)

Health: 6 HP (3 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Swims (water-only mob)
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Hatched from Frogspawn (laid by Frogs)
- Can be found in Swamp and Mangrove Swamp biomes (where frogs lay eggs)

Drops:
- Nothing

Special Abilities:
- Baby form of the Frog
- Eventually grows into a Frog (after ~20 minutes)
- Frog variant determined by biome temperature when the tadpole matures:
  - Warm biome → White Frog
  - Temperate biome → Orange Frog
  - Cold biome → Green Frog
- Can be carried in a Water Bucket (Bucket of Tadpole)
- Dies outside of water — needs water to survive

Behavior:
- Swims aimlessly in water
- Cannot survive on land
- Grows into an adult Frog after time

Sounds: Faint water movement sounds

Notes:
- Carrying tadpoles to different biomes controls which frog color variant they grow into
- This is how players obtain all 3 Frog variants and thus all 3 Froglight types
- Tadpoles in buckets are a renewable resource — place bucket → tadpole grows → frog breeds → more tadpoles
- One of the smallest mobs in the game (alongside silverfish and endermite)
- Added in 1.19 alongside Frogs

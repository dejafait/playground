Name: Endermite
Type: Hostile
Category: Arthropod

Health: 8 HP (4 hearts)
Attack Damage: 2
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.25
Size: Very small (0.4 × 0.3 × 0.4)
XP Dropped: 3

Spawn Locations:
- 15% chance to spawn when a player throws an Ender Pearl
- Very rarely spawns in The End

Drops:
- Nothing

Special Abilities:
- Endermen are naturally hostile to Endermites and will hunt and kill them
  (Used to build Enderman XP farms — Endermite in a minecart attracts Endermen)
- Very small — hard to hit
- Short lifespan — despawns after 2 minutes unless named with a Name Tag

Weaknesses:
- Bane of Arthropods enchantment
- Standard attacks — very low HP

Immunities:
- None

Behavior:
- Attacks players immediately
- Causes nearby Endermen to attack it (mutual hostility)
- Despawns quickly if not named

Sounds: Chittering, similar to silverfish

Notes:
- The Endermite is critical for building Enderman farms in The End
- By placing a named Endermite in a minecart, Endermen are drawn to it and fall into a trap
- Bane of Arthropods enchantment applies
- One of the rarest mobs to find without throwing Ender Pearls
- Despite being tiny, it does respectable damage per hit

Name: Phantom
Type: Hostile
Category: Undead / Flying

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: 6 (Easy) / 6 (Normal) / 12 (Hard)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Flies at 0.50-0.75 (swooping)
Size: Small (0.5 × 0.5 × 0.5) to Large (16× scale)
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Overworld sky (night time)
- Only spawns when the player has not slept or died for 3+ in-game days
- Altitude: Y=80-120 (higher if higher terrain is present)

Drops:
- Phantom Membrane (0-1, up to 4 with Looting III)

Special Abilities:
- Swoops down at the player from high altitude
- Burns in sunlight (only while actively attacking the player)
- Phantoms are scared of Cats (flee from them)
- Spawn in groups of 1-4, scaling with consecutive sleepless nights
- Size varies (1-3 scale variants exist via commands)

Weaknesses:
- Sleeping removes the spawn condition (killing all current Phantoms after a rest)
- Cats — Phantoms flee from them
- Sunlight during their swoop attack

Immunities:
- None specific

Behavior:
- Circles at high altitude
- Swoops down in a curved attack and retreats back up
- Targets the player who hasn't slept
- Multiple spawn and attack in waves

Sounds: Shrieking, flapping wings

Notes:
- Added as the result of a mob vote at MINECON Earth 2017
- Phantom Membranes are used to repair Elytra (critical late-game item)
- Also used in Potions of Slow Falling
- One of the few mobs that punishes the player for poor gameplay habits (not sleeping)
- Cats are the easiest natural deterrent — keep one nearby

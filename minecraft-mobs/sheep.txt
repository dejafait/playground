Name: Sheep
Type: Passive
Category: Animal

Health: 8 HP (4 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.23
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Plains
- Forest
- Mountains
- Most grassy biomes (common)

Drops:
- Mutton (1-2, Raw — Cooked if on fire)
- Wool (1 block, color depends on sheep's color)

Natural Colors:
- White (81.836%)
- Brown (3%)
- Black (3%)
- Gray (3%)
- Light Gray (3%)
- Pink (0.164% — very rare)
- Brown (5%)

Special Mechanics:
- Shear with Shears to get 1-3 Wool without killing the sheep
- Wool regrows after the sheep eats Grass (takes several minutes)
- Sheep can be dyed with any dye — wool and breeding follow the dye color
- Two dyed sheep bred together can produce mixed-color offspring

Breeding:
- Feed 2 Sheep Wheat

Special Abilities:
- Eating Grass Block converts it to Dirt and regrows their wool
- Can be dyed all 16 colors
- Jeb_ name tag makes sheep cycle through all colors (rainbow sheep)
- Brown sheep = brown wool which cannot be made with white wool + dye

Behavior:
- Wanders and grazes
- Eats grass to regrow wool
- Follows players holding Wheat

Sounds: Bleating

Notes:
- Wool is required for Beds (critical early game item — controls night/Phantom spawning)
- Shearing is more efficient than killing — returns more wool over time
- The "Jeb_" easter egg is one of Minecraft's most famous hidden features
- Required for crafting Beds, Banners, Paintings, and Carpets
- Pink sheep are rare and considered lucky by many players

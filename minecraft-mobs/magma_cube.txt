Name: Magma Cube
Type: Hostile
Category: Nether Monster

Health: Large 16 HP / Medium 4 HP / Small 1 HP
Attack Damage: Large 6 / Medium 4 / Small 3
Armor: Large 6 / Medium 3 / Small 0
Movement Speed: Large 0.20 / Medium 0.45 / Small 0.65 (bouncing)
XP Dropped: Large 4 / Medium 2 / Small 1

Spawn Locations:
- Nether (all biomes, common in Basalt Deltas)
- Magma Cube Spawners in Bastion Remnants
- Around Nether Fortress Blazes (occasionally)

Drops:
- Magma Cream (0-1, all sizes)
- Blaze Powder (if Magma Cube is slain in specific circumstances — actually just Magma Cream)

Special Abilities:
- Splits into 2-4 smaller Magma Cubes when killed
- Immune to fire and lava
- Immune to fall damage
- Bounces toward player — jumps high and far
- Fiery outer shell appearance
- Hard exterior gives partial armor

Weaknesses:
- Standard melee/ranged attacks
- Can be difficult to hit while bouncing

Immunities:
- Fire and lava
- Fall damage

Behavior:
- Bounces around the Nether
- Pursues players aggressively
- Splits on death like Slimes
- More dangerous than Slimes due to fire immunity and higher damage

Sounds: Squishy bouncing sounds, fire crackling

Notes:
- Nether equivalent of the Slime
- Magma Cream is essential for Potions of Fire Resistance
- Common in Basalt Deltas where they swarm in large numbers
- Despite being fire-related, the mob itself looks like a lava-filled cube
- Small Magma Cubes still deal 3 damage — unlike Slimes which deal 0

Name: Witch
Type: Hostile
Category: Illager (Humanoid)

Health: 26 HP (13 hearts)
Attack Damage: Splash Potions — varies (Poison, Instant Damage, Slowness, Weakness)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.25
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Overworld (night, light level 0, rarely)
- Witch Huts in Swamp biomes (dedicated spawn)
- Raids (wave 1 onwards)
- Created when a Villager is struck by lightning within 4 blocks

Drops:
- Glowstone Dust (0-2)
- Sugar (0-2)
- Redstone (0-2)
- Spider Eye (0-2)
- Glass Bottle (0-2)
- Gunpowder (0-2)
- Stick (0-2)
- Potions (if holding one when killed — rare)

Special Abilities:
- Throws Splash Potions of Poison, Instant Damage, Slowness, and Weakness at players
- Drinks potions to heal and protect itself:
  - Instant Health when low HP
  - Fire Resistance when on fire
  - Water Breathing when in water
  - Speed when pursuing a target
- Highly resistant to many potion effects
- Does not drink potions when airborne

Weaknesses:
- Instant Damage potions DO affect witches (they are not undead)
- Can be interrupted mid-potion-drink by dealing damage

Immunities:
- Highly resistant to most potion effects (85% resistance)
- Fire Resistance when threatened by fire

Behavior:
- Keeps distance and pelts player with potions
- Self-heals when threatened
- Joins raids as a support unit
- Swamp Hut witches never despawn

Sounds: Cackling, muttering, laughing

Notes:
- Villagers struck by lightning within 4 blocks turn into Witches
- One of the more dangerous overworld mobs due to potion spam
- Swamp Hut witches are tied to their structure and never despawn naturally
- Can be difficult to kill due to self-healing with Instant Health potions

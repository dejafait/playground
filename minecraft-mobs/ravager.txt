Name: Ravager
Type: Hostile
Category: Illager / Beast

Health: 100 HP (50 hearts)
Attack Damage: 12 (Easy) / 18 (Normal) / 26 (Hard)
Armor: 50% damage reduction
Movement Speed: 0.45
XP Dropped: 20

Spawn Locations:
- Raids (wave 3 onwards — ridden by various Illagers)
- Does not spawn anywhere outside of Raids

Drops:
- Saddle (1, guaranteed)

Special Abilities:
- Roars after being stunned — knocks back all nearby entities (1 block radius, massive knockback)
- Destroys most crops and leaves when walking through them
- Stuns when attacked (briefly stops — then roars)
- Can be ridden by Vindicators, Pillagers, and Evokers during raids
- Large knockback on every attack

Weaknesses:
- Cannot easily be blocked with a shield (knockback still applies)
- High HP so requires sustained damage
- Can be stun-locked briefly

Immunities:
- None, but has natural armor/damage reduction

Behavior:
- Charges toward players and villagers
- Roars and pushes away enemies after being hit
- Levels farmland as it runs through crops
- Ridden by other Illagers who continue attacking from its back

Sounds: Loud roaring, stomping

Notes:
- Riding Ravagers (Illagers atop) require killing the rider first to dismount the Ravager
- The saddle it drops is the only guaranteed drop — useful for horses
- Ravagers will flatten entire village farms in seconds
- One of the most visually impressive mobs in the game
- High damage + knockback makes them extremely dangerous in village defense

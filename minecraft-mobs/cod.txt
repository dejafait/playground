Name: Cod
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Aquatic

Health: 3 HP (1.5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Swims in schools
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Ocean (normal)
- Cold Ocean
- Lukewarm Ocean
- Frozen Ocean (rarely)
- Schools of 3-7 fish

Drops:
- Raw Cod (1)
- Bone Meal (rarely)
- Cooked Cod (if killed by fire)

Special Abilities:
- Schools in groups of 3-7
- Can be caught in Water Bucket
- Very common food source
- Fishing Rods can catch Cod from any water source (including non-ocean)

Cod Item:
- Raw Cod: 2 hunger bars, 0.4 saturation
- Cooked Cod: 5 hunger bars, 6 saturation — much better cooked
- Also fed to Dolphins, Cats, Ocelots (trust building), and Polar Bears
- Used to tame Cats and Ocelots (as alternative to Salmon)

Behavior:
- Schools in mid-water depth
- Changes direction as a group

Sounds: Bubbling

Notes:
- One of the most common aquatic mobs
- Important for feeding Dolphins (for treasure-finding mechanic)
- Farming cod (with buckets and automated systems) is an easy food source
- Also used in Fisherman villager trades
- Cooked Cod is decent mid-game food when other sources aren't available

Name: Snow Golem
Type: Passive (utility)
Category: Utility / Construct

Health: 4 HP (2 hearts)
Attack Damage: Snowball knockback only (no actual damage — except to Blazes: 3 damage)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.20
XP Dropped: 0

Crafted By:
- 2 Snow Blocks stacked vertically + Carved Pumpkin or Jack o'Lantern on top

Drops:
- Snowballs (4)

Special Abilities:
- Throws snowballs at hostile mobs continuously
- Leaves a trail of Snow on the ground as it walks (only in biomes that allow snow)
- Snowballs stagger and knock back mobs (useful for pushing mobs off cliffs)
- Snowballs deal 3 damage to Blazes specifically
- Can be sheared to remove pumpkin and reveal its face
- Wearing a pumpkin protects it from Endermen aggro

Weaknesses:
- Takes damage in rain and warm/hot biomes (Savanna, Desert, etc.)
- Takes damage in contact with water
- Very low HP — dies almost instantly to any damage
- Biome-restricted usefulness (melts in warm climates)

Immunities:
- Cold / freezing
- Its own snowballs

Behavior:
- Wanders and automatically throws snowballs at hostile mobs
- Runs away from hostile mobs while pelting them
- Creates snow trail in cold biomes

Sounds: Squeaky/icy sounds

Notes:
- Useful for distraction in Nether (throw into Blaze room — snowballs deal real damage)
- Snow farms (using snow golems on snow-covered terrain)
- Very fragile — not a real combat unit, more of a decoy/utility mob
- The pumpkin head is the default look — shearing reveals a happy snowman face
- Makes good automated Blaze counter-units if protected from hazards

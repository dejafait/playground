Name: Drowned
Type: Hostile
Category: Undead

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: Melee 2-4 / Trident throw 9 (Normal)
Armor: 2
Movement Speed: 0.23 (land) / faster in water
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Oceans (all variants)
- Rivers
- When a Zombie is submerged in water for 30 seconds

Drops:
- Rotten Flesh (0-2)
- Gold Ingot (5% — Bedrock only without looting)
- Trident (8.5% if holding one, Java: 3.75% base — Bedrock: 15%)
- Nautilus Shell (3% if holding one)
- Copper Ingot (Java 1.17+, 11%)

Special Abilities:
- Swims fast underwater
- Throws Tridents (if holding one) with high range and damage
- Slows significantly on land
- Can convert Zombies that drown into more Drowned
- Only Drowned that naturally spawn can hold Tridents (not converted Zombies)

Weaknesses:
- Slower on land
- Smite enchantment (undead)
- Sunlight (burns on land)

Immunities:
- Drowning (breathes underwater indefinitely)

Behavior:
- Lurks underwater and attacks swimmers
- Emerges at night to attack coastal areas
- Throws Trident from range
- Excellent swimmer — hard to escape underwater

Sounds: Gurgling, wet zombie sounds

Notes:
- The aquatic undead variant — most dangerous in water
- Trident farm requires dedicated Drowned farm since only natural spawns carry them
- Copper Ingots (Java) make Drowned farms useful for copper farming
- Converted Drowned (from Zombies) cannot carry Tridents — farming distinction
- Nautilus Shells used to craft Conduit (underwater beacon equivalent)

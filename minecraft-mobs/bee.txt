Name: Bee
Type: Neutral (hostile when hive is disturbed or bee attacked)
Category: Animal / Insect

Health: 10 HP (5 hearts)
Attack Damage: 2 + Poison I for 10 seconds (Normal)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Flies at 0.30
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Flower Forest
- Plains (near Bee Nests)
- Sunflower Plains
- Mangrove Swamp
- Cherry Grove
- Meadow
- Naturally attached to Bee Nests on Oak and Birch trees (5% spawn chance per tree)

Drops:
- Nothing

Honey and Hive Mechanics:
- Bees collect pollen from flowers → bring it back to Bee Nest/Hive → makes Honey
- Hive fills through 5 honey levels (visible by honey dripping from block)
- Level 5: Collect Honey Bottles with Glass Bottle, or Honeycombs with Shears
- Honey Bottle: Food item (6 saturation), also cures Poison
- Honeycomb: Used to craft Beehives, Candles, and Wax Copper Blocks
- Campfire under a hive pacifies bees and allows harvesting without aggro

Pollination:
- Bees with pollen on them that fly over farmland crops accelerate growth by 1 stage
- Important for crop farming speedup

Breeding:
- Feed 2 Bees any Flower

Special Abilities:
- Stings once, loses its stinger, then dies after ~1 minute
- Inflicts Poison I for 10 seconds on sting
- Entire hive becomes hostile if one bee is attacked or hive is disturbed without Silk Touch or campfire

Behavior:
- Collects pollen from flowers in a radius around its hive
- Returns to hive in the evening
- Hostile at night or in rain (returns to hive)
- Swarms and attacks when hive is harmed

Sounds: Buzzing

Notes:
- Bees introduced as part of real-world bee conservation awareness
- Silk Touch pickaxe lets you collect a Bee Nest/Hive with bees inside
- Candles (crafted from Honeycombs + String) are a decorative light source
- One of the few mobs that directly benefits crop farming
- Moving a Bee Nest to a base is a valuable mid-game goal

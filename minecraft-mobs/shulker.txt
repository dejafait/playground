Name: Shulker
Type: Hostile
Category: End Monster

Health: 30 HP (15 hearts) — full shell closed
Attack Damage: Bullet 4 + Levitation II for 10 seconds
Armor: 20 (shell closed) / 0 (shell open)
Movement Speed: Stationary (teleports)
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- End Cities (The End, outer islands)
- Attached to End City walls, floors, and ceilings

Drops:
- Shulker Shell (0-1, up to 2 with Looting — 50% base chance)

Special Abilities:
- Hidden inside a shell — immune to almost all damage when closed
- Opens shell to fire homing bullets at players
- Bullets apply Levitation II for 10 seconds (player floats uncontrollably)
- Levitation can send player extremely high → fall damage risk
- When at low health (below 50%), may teleport to a new location on a nearby surface
- Can teleport to the underside of blocks or walls

Weaknesses:
- Only takes damage when shell is open (brief window)
- Bullets can be deflected with shields
- Eggs, snowballs, and other projectiles can hit the body when open

Immunities:
- All damage when shell is closed
- Projectiles when closed

Behavior:
- Clings to surfaces in End Cities
- Peeks open to track nearby players
- Fires homing bullet when fully open
- Closes when hit, then relocates if low health

Sounds: Mechanical clicking/clunking sounds

Notes:
- Shulker Shells are crafted into Shulker Boxes (the most important storage item in the game)
- Shulker Boxes hold 27 items and keep their inventory when broken
- The Levitation effect is deadly near tall structures — Elytra strongly recommended in End Cities
- End Ships (near End Cities) contain an Elytra — the most valuable late-game item
- Farming Shulker Shells is essential for any serious inventory management

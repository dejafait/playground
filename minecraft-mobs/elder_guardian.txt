Name: Elder Guardian
Type: Hostile
Category: Aquatic Monster / Mini-Boss

Health: 80 HP (40 hearts)
Attack Damage: Laser 12 / Thorns 2 (melee retaliation)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Swims at ~0.30
XP Dropped: 10
Count per Monument: 3 (one per wing + one in top room)

Spawn Locations:
- Ocean Monuments (3 per monument — fixed spawn, does not respawn)

Drops:
- Wet Sponge (1, guaranteed)
- Prismarine Shard (0-2)
- Prismarine Crystal (0-1)
- Raw Cod (0-1)
- Fish (various)

Special Abilities:
- Inflicts Mining Fatigue III on all nearby players every 60 seconds
  (Causes player screen to show a ghostly Elder Guardian image)
- Mining Fatigue III makes breaking blocks nearly 5x slower (prevents looting monument quickly)
- Stronger laser attack than regular Guardians
- Thorns retaliation damage on melee hits
- Does not despawn (like regular Guardians in a monument)

Weaknesses:
- Milk cures Mining Fatigue temporarily (~3 seconds before re-affliction)
- Same laser interruption mechanic as Guardian
- Ranged attacks avoid Thorns retaliation

Immunities:
- Drowning

Behavior:
- Patrols specific rooms in the Ocean Monument
- Periodically afflicts all nearby players with Mining Fatigue III
- Fires laser at players in range
- Fixed to specific locations in the Monument

Sounds: Deeper, more resonant version of Guardian sounds

Notes:
- 3 Elder Guardians must be killed to fully "clear" an Ocean Monument
- Wet Sponges they drop are essential for draining the monument for building
- Mining Fatigue III is the defining mechanic that makes monuments so difficult
- One of the most architecturally interesting structures in Minecraft
- After all 3 are killed, the Monument is safe for building/farming

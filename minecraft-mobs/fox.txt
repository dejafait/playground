Name: Fox
Type: Neutral (flees from players — attacks Chickens, Rabbits, Fish)
Category: Animal

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: 2 (hunting) / varies
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.30 (0.60 sprinting)
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Taiga
- Giant Tree Taiga
- Snowy Taiga
- Bamboo Jungle (very rare)

Variants:
- Red Fox (Taiga biomes)
- Arctic Fox / White Fox (Snowy Taiga)

Drops:
- Whatever item it is currently holding in its mouth

Special Abilities:
- Holds items in its mouth (picks up dropped items from the ground)
- Pounces on prey (chickens, rabbits, fish, baby turtles)
- Nocturnal — sleeps during day (under a tree or in shade)
- Trusted: Two foxes bred with Sweet Berries will produce a baby fox that trusts the player
  (Will not flee from the player who bred it, and will defend them)
- Can jump over fences
- Eats Sweet Berries from bushes (takes damage from spiky bushes but still eats them)
- Immune to the effects of certain foods/effects while eating

Trusted Foxes:
- Baby foxes born from player-bred parents naturally trust that player
- Will not run from player, will attack mobs that attack the player
- Cannot breed more trusted foxes from a trusted fox + wild fox

Behavior:
- Flees from players (wild foxes)
- Sleeps during the day, hunts at night
- Sneaks up on prey before pouncing
- Picks up dropped items it finds
- Eats held food items over time

Sounds: Chirping, barking, sneaking sounds

Notes:
- One of Minecraft's most charming passive mobs — sleeping foxes are iconic
- Item-holding mechanic makes them interesting for picking up enchanted items left on the ground
- The only way to get a player-following fox is through breeding (2 generations)
- Arctic Fox variant is visually distinctive in snowy biomes
- Sometimes found sleeping in groups of 2-4

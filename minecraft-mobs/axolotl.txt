Name: Axolotl
Type: Passive (attacks aquatic hostile mobs)
Category: Animal / Aquatic

Health: 14 HP (7 hearts)
Attack Damage: 2 (bite)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Fast swimmer
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Lush Caves biome (underground water bodies)
- Requires clay blocks underneath to spawn

Color Variants (5):
- Lucy (pink) — most common
- Wild (brown) — common
- Gold (yellow) — common
- Cyan (blue-green) — common
- Blue (rare — 1/1200 chance) — extremely rare

Special Abilities:
- Plays Dead: When damaged, may enter "playing dead" state (1 HP, 1/3 chance)
  — Regenerates 4 HP over 10 seconds while playing dead
- Gives Regeneration I and removes Mining Fatigue when player kills a mob the Axolotl is attacking
- Targets and attacks: Fish, Squid, Glow Squid, Drowned, Guardians, Elder Guardians
- Can be scooped up in a Bucket of Water and transported
- Needs to stay moist — takes damage in dry areas outside water (but can survive briefly)
- Can be bred with Tropical Fish

Carrying Axolotls:
- Scoop with Water Bucket → Bucket of Axolotl
- Transport anywhere and release in new water

Behavior:
- Swims in Lush Cave waters
- Attacks any aquatic mobs (Drowned, Guardians, fish) on sight
- Plays dead when near death
- Washes ashore and flops around on land temporarily

Sounds: Gurgling, splashing

Notes:
- Inspired by the real axolotl (critically endangered amphibian native to Mexico)
- Blue axolotls represent real-world rare mutations
- One of the best Ocean Monument raid companions — they tank Guardians and give player Regeneration
- Unlimited Axolotl + bucket = portable fish-killing army
- Added in 1.17 as part of the Caves and Cliffs update

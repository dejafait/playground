Name: Tropical Fish
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Aquatic

Health: 3 HP (1.5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Swims in schools
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Warm Ocean
- Lukewarm Ocean
- Lush Caves (underground water)

Variants:
- Over 2,700 unique visual combinations
- 2 body shapes × 6 patterns × 15 colors = hundreds of combinations
- 22 pre-named varieties (with specific names like Clownfish, Parrotfish, etc.)
- Pattern + color combinations stored in NBT data

Drops:
- Tropical Fish (1 — the food item)
- Bone Meal (rarely)

Special Abilities:
- School behavior — swim in groups
- Can be caught in a Water Bucket (Bucket of Tropical Fish)
- Each bucket retains the specific fish's variant/color

Tropical Fish Item Uses:
- Food (1 hunger bar, low saturation — not great food)
- Feeding Axolotls (the ONLY way to breed Axolotls — Axolotls eat Tropical Fish)
- Decoration in Aquariums

Behavior:
- Swims in schools, direction changes together
- Schools of same pattern/color

Sounds: Bubbling, swimming sounds

Notes:
- 2,700+ variants makes Tropical Fish one of the most visually diverse mobs
- Axolotl breeding requires Tropical Fish — the most important use of the mob
- Bucket of Tropical Fish preserves the exact color/pattern — collectible aspect
- One of the most colorful biome indicators (Warm Oceans only)
- The variety of patterns is procedural — each world has all patterns available

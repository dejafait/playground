Name: Cat
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Tameable

Health: 10 HP (5 hearts)
Attack Damage: 3 (attacks hostile small mobs)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.30
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Villages (1 per 4 beds, up to 10 per village)
- Witch Huts in Swamps (1 black cat always present)

Drops:
- String (rarely)
- Gift when owner wakes (see below)

Variants (11 types):
- Tabby, Tuxedo, Red, Siamese, British Shorthair, Calico, Persian, Ragdoll, White, Jellie, Black

Taming:
- Tame with Raw Cod or Raw Salmon (multiple needed — random chance)
- Must approach slowly (crouching helps)
- Collar color can be changed with dye

Special Abilities:
- Creepers actively avoid Cats (will not approach within 6 blocks)
- Phantoms actively avoid Cats (will not swoop near them)
- Brings owner a gift each morning when owner wakes from bed:
  Possible gifts: Rabbit's Foot, Rabbit Hide, String, Rotten Flesh, Feather,
                  Raw Chicken, Phantom Membrane, Mouse (raw)
- Sits on beds and chests (can block chest access/sleep)
- Purrs when near owner
- Follows owner like a tamed wolf

Behavior:
- Skittish around players — approach slowly
- Teleports to owner if too far away (like wolves)
- Sits down on beds and chests autonomously
- Hisses at Creepers and Phantoms (actually scares them away)

Sounds: Meowing, purring, hissing

Notes:
- Passive defense against Creepers — a cat near your base prevents sneaky Creeper deaths
- Phantom counter — sleep with a cat nearby to prevent Phantom spawning
- 11 total variants — one (Jellie) named after a real cat owned by a Minecraft developer
- Unlike Wolves, Cats generally don't fight mobs (they just scare specific ones)
- One of the most useful passive utility pets in the game

Name: Wither Skeleton
Type: Hostile
Category: Undead

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: 5 (all difficulties) + Wither effect for 10 seconds
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.25
Height: 2.4 blocks (taller than doorways)
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Nether Fortresses exclusively
- Light level 7 or below in Nether Fortresses

Drops:
- Bones (0-2)
- Coal (0-1)
- Wither Skeleton Skull (2.5% chance — increased by Looting)
- Stone Sword (8.5%)

Special Abilities:
- Inflicts Wither status effect (drains health over 10 seconds, turns hearts black)
- Tall enough that standard 2-block doorways don't stop them
- Immune to fire and lava
- Burns sunlight doesn't affect them (Nether mob)
- Wither Skull drops are essential to summon the Wither boss

Weaknesses:
- Smite enchantment (undead — bonus damage)
- Looting enchantment greatly improves skull drop rate

Immunities:
- Fire and lava
- The Wither effect itself
- Does not take fire damage from the environment

Behavior:
- Patrols Nether Fortresses
- Pursues players on sight
- Will duck through certain low openings
- Travels in groups in fortress corridors

Sounds: Bone rattling (similar to regular skeleton)

Notes:
- Farming Wither Skeleton Skulls is one of the most tedious grinds in the game
- 3 skulls are needed to summon the Wither boss (for the Nether Star → Beacon)
- Looting III sword brings skull drop rate from 2.5% to 5.5%
- Beheading enchantment (Bedrock) further increases skull drop chance
- Taller than standard skeletons — can't walk under 2-block clearances easily

Name: Wandering Trader
Type: Passive
Category: Humanoid / NPC

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.50
XP Dropped: 0
Despawn Timer: Disappears after 40-60 minutes (2-3 in-game days)

Spawn Locations:
- Random locations near the player in the Overworld
- Spawns every 1 in-game day (24 min), tries up to 10 times to find valid location
- Likely to appear within 48 blocks of player's spawn or bed

Drops:
- Leads (2, from its 2 llamas when killed)
- Blue dye (1, from drinking invisibility potion — it drops the bottle)

Always Accompanied By:
- 2 Trader Llamas (leashed to it)

Trade Inventory (random selection, 6 trades total):
- Rare plants, flowers, saplings (unique biome plants)
- Coral blocks
- Blue ice
- Packed ice
- Sea grass
- Podzol
- Various dyes
- Sand, Red Sand, Gravel
- Nautilus Shell

Special Abilities:
- Drinks Invisibility Potion at night (becomes invisible — llamas still visible)
- Trades items that may not be available in the local biome
- Unique source of certain biome-specific items

Weaknesses:
- No combat ability
- Attacked by Illagers (Pillagers target them specifically)

Behavior:
- Wanders the overworld
- Offers random 6 trades per visit
- Becomes invisible at night
- Disappears after timer expires

Sounds: "Hmm" sounds (like villager)

Notes:
- Trades are locked (no leveling system unlike regular Villagers)
- Most useful for biome-locked items players can't easily reach
- The only natural source of certain decorative plants
- Wandering Traders are not worth killing — Leads are better obtained via sheep/fences
- Occasionally wanders into dangerous situations and dies (attacked by Pillagers)

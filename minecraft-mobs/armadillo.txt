Name: Armadillo
Type: Passive
Category: Animal

Health: 12 HP (6 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.14
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Savanna
- Savanna Plateau
- Windswept Savanna
- Badlands biomes (sparse)

Drops:
- Armadillo Scute (1, occasionally while alive and when rolled and hit)

Scute Drops:
- Periodically drops Armadillo Scutes while wandering (every few minutes, passive)
- Additional Scutes dropped when curled up and hit (but this harms the mob — not recommended)

Special Abilities:
- Rolls into a Ball: When threatened by:
  - Player sprinting nearby
  - Undead mobs (Zombies, Skeletons, etc.)
  - Player attacks
  → Curls into armored ball — reduces incoming damage significantly
- Unrolls when threat leaves the area
- Scute Drops: Passively drops Armadillo Scutes over time (similar to Chicken laying Eggs)

Wolf Armor:
- Armadillo Scutes (6) + Crafting Table = Wolf Armor
- Wolf Armor can be equipped on tamed Wolves
- Provides significant damage reduction for pet wolves
- Repairs with additional Armadillo Scutes

Breeding:
- Feed 2 Armadillos Spider Eyes

Behavior:
- Slow and peaceful
- Curls up when frightened
- Drops Scutes passively
- Uncurls when safe

Sounds: Clicking, shuffling sounds

Notes:
- Won the Minecraft Live 2023 Mob Vote
- Added in 1.21 (Tricky Trials update)
- The Wolf Armor mechanic is the primary reason Armadillos were added
- One of the few mobs that passively generates a resource (like Chickens laying Eggs)
- Real armadillos do roll into balls for protection — accurately represented
- Scutes only come from natural drops — cannot be sheared or harvested aggressively

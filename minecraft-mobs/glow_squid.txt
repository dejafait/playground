Name: Glow Squid
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Aquatic

Health: 10 HP (5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Swims at ~0.20
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Underground water sources (below Y=30)
- Lush Caves (water areas)
- Rarely in deep ocean sections

Drops:
- Glow Ink Sac (1-3)

Special Abilities:
- Emits a glowing effect (bioluminescent)
- Squirts glowing ink when attacked (same visual behavior as Squid)
- Loses glow momentarily when hurt (dims and regains)

Glow Ink Sac Uses:
- Sign text: Apply to Sign text to make it glow (visible in darkness)
- Item Frames: Apply to Item Frame to make items displayed inside glow
- Crafting: Used in crafting Glow Item Frame

Behavior:
- Swims aimlessly in underground water
- Visual glow effect makes them easy to spot in dark caves
- Same basic behavior as regular Squid

Sounds: Squirting sounds (when hit)

Notes:
- Won the Minecraft Live 2020 Mob Vote (beating Iceologer and Moobloom)
- Added in Java Edition 1.17
- The glow effect is purely visual — does not emit real light block
- Glow Ink Sacs are purely cosmetic use items (signs, item frames)
- One of the most visually appealing mobs in cave environments
- Named after the real-world phenomenon of bioluminescent squid

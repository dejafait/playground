Name: Dolphin
Type: Neutral (attacks if provoked or fed and not followed)
Category: Animal / Aquatic

Health: 10 HP (5 hearts)
Attack Damage: 3 (Normal)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Fast swimmer (~0.45-0.65)
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Warm Ocean
- Lukewarm Ocean
- Cool Ocean
- Normal Ocean
(NOT in Frozen Oceans or Deep Frozen Oceans)

Drops:
- Raw Cod (0-1)

Special Abilities:
- Dolphin's Grace: Swimming near a Dolphin gives the player Dolphin's Grace status effect
  (30% faster swimming speed — allows faster ocean navigation)
- Treasure Hunting: Feeding a Dolphin Raw Cod or Raw Salmon makes it swim toward the nearest
  buried treasure, shipwreck, or underwater ruin (Lead mechanic)
- Jumps out of water elegantly (aesthetic behavior)
- Needs air — breathes above water surface like real dolphins
- Suffocates if kept out of water too long
- Will become hostile if not fed after being fed (timer expires)

Behavior:
- Playful — jumps out of water
- Follows boats occasionally
- Swims near surface for air
- Hostile if provoked or if the player doesn't follow through after feeding
- Plays with items dropped in water

Sounds: Clicking, chattering, whistling (dolphin-like echolocation sounds)

Notes:
- The treasure-hunting mechanic makes Dolphins incredibly useful for finding ocean loot
- Dolphin's Grace stacks with Speed potions for extreme swim speed
- Cannot be tamed — you can interact but not keep one as a permanent companion
- Will die if kept out of the ocean (needs both water and air access)
- One of the most beloved ocean mobs for both aesthetics and utility

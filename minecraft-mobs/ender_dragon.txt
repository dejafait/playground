Name: Ender Dragon
Type: Boss
Category: Boss Mob

Health: 200 HP (100 hearts)
Attack Damage: Head melee 15 (Easy) / 22 (Normal) / 30 (Hard)
              Dragon's Breath (area damage + Dragon's Breath effect) 6/s
              Wing hit 5 (Easy) / 7 (Normal) / 10 (Hard)
Armor: Immune to most damage except when attacking/at head
Movement Speed: Flies at high speed — varies
XP Dropped: 12,000 (first kill) / 500 (subsequent kills)

Spawn Location:
- The End (main island, The End dimension)
- Respawned by placing 4 End Crystals on the Exit Portal edges

Drops:
- Dragon Egg (1, first kill only — decorative)
- Opens the Exit Portal (first kill — reveals End poem)
- 12,000 XP on first kill (enough for several levels)

Special Abilities:
- Immune to most damage except:
  1. Bow shots and ranged hits at its head while it perches at the portal
  2. Melee/ranged hits anywhere while it swoops or perches
- Healed by End Crystals on top of pillars — must be destroyed first
- Dragon's Breath attack: leaves a lingering cloud that drains HP
- Charge attack: flies straight through the player (wing and body damage)
- Fireball: shoots purple acid fireballs
- Destroys most blocks it flies through (except End Stone, obsidian, bedrock)
- Can only be hit in the head for full damage

Strategy:
1. Destroy all End Crystals on obsidian pillars (use bow or bed explosions)
2. Shoot it with bow while it hovers at the center portal or perches
3. Avoid dragon breath clouds and swooping charges

End Crystals: Regenerate the Dragon's HP when within range — priority targets

Immunities:
- All potion effects
- Fire
- Cannot be killed by Fall damage (flies)
- Damage to body is heavily reduced — head is the weak point

Behavior:
- Circles the main island constantly
- Periodically swoops toward the player
- Perches on Exit Portal to breathe fire
- Charges in a straight line at the player periodically

Sounds: Roaring, wing flapping, growling

Notes:
- The intended "final boss" of Minecraft — killing it finishes the main game narrative
- The Dragon Egg is the rarest decorative block (only 1 per world on first kill)
- Dragon's Breath (collect with glass bottles while standing in it) is used for Lingering Potions
- The End Poem (after death) is one of gaming's most celebrated pieces of writing
- Can be re-summoned indefinitely by crafting new End Crystals

Name: Polar Bear
Type: Neutral (hostile when cub is present)
Category: Animal

Health: 30 HP (15 hearts)
Attack Damage: 10 (Easy) / 18 (Normal) / 27 (Hard)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.25 (land) / faster in water
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Frozen Ocean
- Deep Frozen Ocean
- Snowy Tundra
- Snowy Mountains
- Ice Spikes
- Always spawn in groups that may include cubs

Drops:
- Raw Cod (0-2)
- Raw Salmon (0-2)

Behavior:
- Neutral when alone (adult without cub nearby)
- Immediately attacks if player approaches a cub
- Rears up on hind legs before attacking (visual warning)
- Adult will attack mobs that attack its cub
- Can swim effectively in frozen oceans

Special Abilities:
- One of the hardest-hitting neutral mobs when provoked
- Rears up on hind legs (animation signals incoming aggro)
- Cubs cannot be tamed or bred by the player

Weaknesses:
- Standard weapons — just avoid provoking near cubs

Immunities:
- Cold / freezing effects

Notes:
- Can fish (break ice blocks to fish) — one of the few mobs with tool use-like behavior
- Very dangerous if accidentally walked near a cub
- Good source of fish drops in ice biomes
- Baby polar bears (cubs) are among the cutest mob variants
- Adult polar bears have the highest neutral-mob damage — nearly boss-tier when angered

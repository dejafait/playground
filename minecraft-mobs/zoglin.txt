Name: Zoglin
Type: Hostile
Category: Undead

Health: 40 HP (20 hearts)
Attack Damage: 6 (Easy) / 10 (Normal) / 15 (Hard) — Adult / Baby deals less
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.30
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Created when a Hoglin enters the Overworld or The End
- Does not spawn naturally

Drops:
- Rotten Flesh (2-4)

Special Abilities:
- Zombified version of a Hoglin
- Attacks nearly all mobs and players indiscriminately
- Cannot be bred or made passive (unlike Hoglin)
- Immune to most status effects as an undead mob
- Cannot be distracted by Piglins (unlike Hoglins)
- Knocks back targets on hit

Weaknesses:
- Smite enchantment (undead mob — takes bonus damage)
- Standard combat

Immunities:
- Fire and lava
- Poison (undead immunity)
- Regen potions don't heal them
- Does not flee from Warped Fungus (unlike Hoglin)

Behavior:
- Attacks everything it can reach — players, mobs, villagers
- Completely hostile with no passive trigger
- Baby Zoglins also exist and are also hostile (unlike baby Hoglins)

Sounds: Undead/zombie-like versions of Hoglin sounds

Notes:
- The Overworld equivalent of a Hoglin, but far more dangerous
- Unlike Hoglins, nothing calms a Zoglin down
- Attacks even other hostile mobs (can be used for mob infighting)
- Rare to encounter since Hoglins rarely enter the Overworld naturally
- Baby Zoglins are an unusual exception to baby mobs being passive

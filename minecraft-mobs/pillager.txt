Name: Pillager
Type: Hostile
Category: Illager

Health: 24 HP (12 hearts)
Attack Damage: Crossbow 4-5 (Normal)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.35
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Pillager Outposts (dedicated structure)
- Illager Patrols (roaming overworld groups)
- Raids (all waves)

Drops:
- Crossbow (8.5%, may be enchanted)
- Arrows (0-2)
- Emerald (0-1, during Raids)
- Dark Oak Log (0-1, when carrying banner — Patrol Captain)

Special Abilities:
- Ranged crossbow attacks with good range and accuracy
- Patrol Captains carry the Illager Banner — killing one gives Bad Omen status effect
  (Bad Omen triggers a Raid when entering a village)
- Reload animation — brief vulnerability while reloading crossbow
- Attacks villagers, iron golems, wandering traders, and players

Weaknesses:
- Closing distance forces melee switch (crossbow is close-range awkward)
- Shield blocks crossbow bolts entirely
- While reloading

Immunities:
- None

Behavior:
- Patrols and guards Pillager Outposts
- Roams in Patrol groups (2-5 Pillagers)
- Shoots from range, retreats when player closes in
- Patrol leaders hold Illager Banner overhead

Sounds: Crossbow loading, villager-like grunts

Notes:
- Killing a Patrol Captain = Bad Omen → triggers Raid on next village entry (intentional mechanic)
- Raids give Hero of the Village status (discounted villager trades)
- Pillager Outposts are the first major Illager threat players encounter
- Crossbow can be enchanted — Multishot and Piercing are powerful on them
- Wandering Traders are also targets — explaining why they're sometimes found dead

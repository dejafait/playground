Name: Panda
Type: Neutral (can be aggressive if attacked)
Category: Animal

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: 6 (if attacked or aggressive type)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.15 (slow — lazy variant even slower)
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Bamboo Jungle
- Sparse Jungle (rarely, requires nearby bamboo)

Drops:
- Bamboo (1)
- Slimeball (occasionally when sneezing — baby pandas)

Personality Types (genetic system):
Each panda has a hidden and expressed gene determining personality:
- Normal: Nothing special
- Lazy: Lies on its back, won't follow player even with food, slowest
- Worried: Trembles in rain, scared of hostile mobs, won't eat when player nearby
- Playful: Rolls and somersaults randomly
- Aggressive: Attacks if provoked, continues attacking for a while
- Weak: Sneezes more often, low HP variant
- Brown: Rare recessive gene — gives brown fur coloring

Breeding:
- Requires at least 8 Bamboo blocks within 5 blocks of both pandas
- Feed both pandas Bamboo to enter love mode
- Baby pandas exist — sneeze occasionally (can drop Slimeballs)
- Personality is inherited (genetic system behind the scenes)

Behavior:
- Eats Bamboo and Cake items it finds
- Aggressive type attacks if any panda in area is harmed
- Lazy type lies on its back periodically
- Playful type rolls around randomly
- Baby pandas sneeze and sometimes startle adults into defensive behavior

Sounds: Squeaking, sneezing, munching bamboo

Notes:
- One of the more complex passive mob systems — genetics determine personality
- Brown pandas are very rare (both genes must be Brown — recessive)
- Pandas are endangered in the real world — reflected in their rare spawn and complex breeding requirements
- Baby panda sneezes are among the most adorable things in Minecraft
- One of the most complex mob behavior systems in the game

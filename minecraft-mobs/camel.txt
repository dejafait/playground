Name: Camel
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Rideable

Health: 32 HP (16 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.09 (walk) / fast dash ability
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Desert Villages (1 per village, in the center)
- Does not spawn in the wild — only in Desert Villages

Drops:
- Nothing

Riding:
- Place a Saddle to ride
- Two players can ride simultaneously (one on each side of the hump)
- No steering item needed — controlled with WASD

Special Abilities:
- Two-Player Mount: Unique among rideable mobs — both players can sit on one Camel
- Dash: Press Jump twice rapidly to dash forward (boost speed, cooldown applies)
  — Dash crosses gaps that would be impassable at normal speed
- Height Advantage: Camels are very tall — most hostile mobs cannot reach the rider
  (Zombies, Skeletons, most surface mobs cannot hit players on Camel)
- Sits Down: Camels periodically sit down (kneel) — right-click while sitting to make it stand

Breeding:
- Feed 2 Camels Cacti

Behavior:
- Wanders Desert Villages
- Periodically kneels/sits
- Follows players holding Cacti
- Baby Camels are smaller but have same proportions

Sounds: Humming, grunting, sand movement sounds

Notes:
- Added in 1.20 (Trails and Tales update) as the Desert Village mount
- The two-player riding mechanic makes it perfect for co-op exploration
- Height protection from mobs is a unique defensive advantage
- The dash mechanic allows crossing gaps and moving quickly across open desert
- Cacti as breeding item is fitting — the only rideable mob bred with a hostile-to-player plant

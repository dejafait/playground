Name: Creeper
Type: Hostile
Category: Monster

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: Explosion damage 49 (Normal, direct hit) — varies greatly with distance
Explosion Power: 3 (normal) / 6 (charged)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.25
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Overworld (night, light level 0)
- Caves and dark areas

Drops:
- Gunpowder (0-2)
- Music Disc (Cat or 13) when killed by a Skeleton's arrow
- Creeper Head (when killed by charged creeper explosion)

Special Abilities:
- Silently approaches the player
- Hisses for 1.5 seconds then explodes
- Charged by lightning strikes — doubles explosion power
- Explosion destroys terrain (one of the few mobs that can)
- Does not explode if player moves away in time (timer resets)

Weaknesses:
- Cats and Ocelots — Creepers flee from them
- Fire causes premature explosion sometimes
- Backing away resets the fuse

Immunities:
- None specific, but immune to their own explosion damage

Behavior:
- Wanders silently
- Approaches player within 3 blocks before activating fuse
- If player moves away before explosion, fuse resets
- Runs from Cats and Ocelots

Sounds: Hissing before explosion, no footstep sounds (intentional — originally a bug)

Notes:
- One of the most iconic Minecraft mobs
- Originally created by Notch as a failed pig model
- The silent approach makes it notoriously dangerous
- Charged Creepers are rare and extremely deadly
- Creeper faces appear on TNT, maps, and in game iconography

Name: Chicken
Type: Passive
Category: Animal

Health: 4 HP (2 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.25
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Plains
- Forest
- Most grassy biomes (common)
- Hatches from thrown Eggs (1/8 chance per egg, 1/32 for 4 chickens)

Drops:
- Raw Chicken (1, Cooked if on fire)
- Feather (0-2)
- Egg (occasionally — not on death, but passively while alive)

Breeding:
- Feed 2 Chickens Seeds (any type: Wheat Seeds, Melon Seeds, Pumpkin Seeds, Beetroot Seeds)

Special Abilities:
- Lays Eggs every 5-10 minutes (random interval)
- Floats when falling (flaps wings) — takes no fall damage
- Baby chickens can hatch from thrown Eggs
- Eggs can be thrown to potentially spawn baby chickens:
  12.5% chance of 1 chick, 1/32 chance of 4 chicks

Baby Chickens:
- Hatch from Eggs thrown at blocks
- "Chicken Jockey" — Baby Zombies and Baby Husks have a 5% chance to spawn riding a Chicken
  (Chicken Jockeys are extremely fast — more dangerous than regular baby zombies)

Behavior:
- Wanders and pecks at ground
- Follows players holding Seeds
- Floats down from heights safely
- Clucks while walking

Sounds: Clucking, squawking when hurt

Notes:
- Chickens are one of the most efficient food mobs (lay eggs passively)
- Chicken farms (hopper + egg collection + lava kill) are excellent starter farms
- Feathers are needed for Arrows (critical crafting item)
- Raw Chicken has a 30% chance to give Food Poisoning — always cook it
- Chicken Jockeys are rare but terrifying in a cave ambush

Name: Evoker
Type: Hostile
Category: Illager / Spell Caster

Health: 24 HP (12 hearts)
Attack Damage: Vex (13 on Normal) / Fang row (6 per fang)
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.50
XP Dropped: 10

Spawn Locations:
- Woodland Mansions (rare — specific rooms only)
- Raids (waves 5-7)

Drops:
- Totem of Undying (1, guaranteed — most valuable mob drop in the game)
- Emerald (0-1, during Raids)

Special Abilities:
- Summons Vexes (3 at a time) which fly through blocks and attack
- Summons a line or circle of Evoker Fangs from the ground
- Can change the color of Blue Sheep to Red (casting a "wololo" effect)
- During raids, the most dangerous unit
- Low HP for a spellcaster — fragile but lethal

Weaknesses:
- Closing the distance quickly before it can cast
- Ranged attacks

Immunities:
- None

Behavior:
- Keeps distance and casts spells
- Periodically summons 3 Vexes
- Summons Fang attacks in a line toward the player or in a ring if too close
- Retreats while casting

Sounds: Spell casting sounds, villager-like muttering, ominous chanting

Notes:
- The ONLY source of the Totem of Undying in survival mode
- Totems of Undying save the player from death once (one of the most powerful items)
- Found in Woodland Mansions (rare) — important to locate one early-game
- Evokers are the leader-tier Illagers — higher XP than most mobs
- The sheep-color spell is a reference to Age of Empires' Priests ("Wololo")

Name: Parrot
Type: Passive
Category: Animal / Tameable

Health: 6 HP (3 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: Flies at 0.50
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Jungle
- Bamboo Jungle
- Sparse Jungle

Color Variants (5):
- Red
- Blue
- Green
- Cyan
- Gray

Taming:
- Feed Seeds (Wheat, Melon, Pumpkin, or Beetroot seeds) — random chance per seed
- Multiple seeds usually needed
- Once tamed, perches on player's shoulder when close

Special Abilities:
- Mimics nearby mob sounds — plays sounds of mobs within ~20 blocks
  (Great for detecting hidden Creepers or Zombies nearby!)
- Sits on player's shoulder (both shoulders can have a parrot each)
- Dances when near a Music Disc playing in a Jukebox
- Can be ordered to sit or follow (like wolf)
- DEADLY with Cookies: feeding a Cookie instantly kills the parrot
  (This is actually educational — real parrots are fatally sensitive to xylitol)

Shoulder Mechanics:
- Jump or fall to dismount the parrot from your shoulder
- Parrots on shoulders see what you see (their POV follows yours)

Behavior:
- Flies and hovers near player
- Mimics mob sounds as a proximity warning system
- Dances to Jukebox music
- Sits on player shoulders

Sounds: Squawking, mimicked mob sounds, dancing chirps

Notes:
- The mimic ability is genuinely useful — a parrot squawking a Creeper sound means danger nearby
- The Cookie mechanic is one of Minecraft's rare real-world educational touches
- Both shoulder slots can hold a parrot simultaneously (one on each shoulder)
- One of the more interactive and charming companion mobs
- 5 variants covering most of the bright color spectrum

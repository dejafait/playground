Name: Wither
Type: Boss
Category: Boss Mob / Undead

Health: 300 HP (150 hearts) — Java Edition
        600 HP (300 hearts) — Bedrock Edition
Attack Damage: Wither Skull (blue/black) 8 + Wither II effect
              Blue Wither Skull: 12 + Wither II + cannot be deflected + destroys blocks
              Dash Attack: 15 (Bedrock only, below half health)
Armor: Natural armor equivalent to 4 armor
Movement Speed: Flies
XP Dropped: 50

Summoned By:
- Player-crafted: 4 Soul Sand / Soul Soil in T-shape + 3 Wither Skeleton Skulls on top
- Can be summoned in any dimension

Drops:
- Nether Star (1, guaranteed)
- 50 XP

Phases:
Phase 1 (Full HP):
- Shoots both black and blue Wither Skulls
- Can be damaged normally

Phase 2 (Below 50% HP, Java Edition):
- Gains a "Wither Armor" shield — immune to projectiles
- Must be killed with melee weapons
- Attacks more frequently
- (Bedrock) Gains new Dash attack and spawns Wither Skeletons

Special Abilities:
- Wither Effect: Turns hearts black, drains HP over time — milk cures it
- Blue Wither Skulls: Explosive, bypass shields, destroy blocks (obsidian-proof structures stop normal skulls)
- Destroys all blocks (except bedrock and end portal frames) with contact and explosions
- Immune to fire, lava, drowning, and all potion effects
- Heals from dealing damage to undead mobs (in Bedrock)
- Summons Wither Skeletons (Bedrock, phase 2)

Construction Recipe:
Row 1: Soul Sand, Soul Sand, Soul Sand
Row 2: Wither Skull, Wither Skull, Wither Skull
(Must be in 3D T-shape formation, skulls on top — in world, not crafting table)

Immunities:
- Fire and lava
- All potion effects
- Drowning
- Suffocation damage

Weaknesses:
- Beacon beam (Java: damages and weakens)
- Melee weapons in Phase 2 (projectile immune)
- Can be pinned underground where it can't fly well

Behavior:
- Explosion on spawn that destroys surrounding area (warning: don't spawn in your base)
- Flies around targeting players and mobs
- Prioritizes attacking living mobs and players
- Destroys terrain as it moves

Sounds: Eerie moaning, explosion sounds, boss music

Notes:
- One of the two bosses in Minecraft (along with the Ender Dragon)
- The Nether Star is used to craft the Beacon — one of the most powerful utility blocks
- Spawning in the Nether underground (bedrock ceiling) is a popular strategy
- Blue Skull + obsidian box is the safest overworld kill method
- The Wither is often considered harder than the Ender Dragon on Java

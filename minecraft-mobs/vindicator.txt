Name: Vindicator
Type: Hostile
Category: Illager

Health: 24 HP (12 hearts)
Attack Damage: 13 (Easy) / 19 (Normal) / 25 (Hard) — axe melee
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.35
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Woodland Mansions (common inside)
- Raids (waves 2-7, increasing numbers)

Drops:
- Emerald (0-1, rare during raid)
- Iron Axe (8.5%, may be enchanted)

Special Abilities:
- Extremely high melee damage — one of the strongest melee mobs
- Part of the Illager faction — attacks villagers, iron golems, and players
- During Raids, a named Vindicator called "Johnny" will attack ALL mobs (not just players/villagers)
  — includes other hostile mobs and even other Illagers
- Uses an Iron Axe which bypasses shields partially
- Breaks down doors during Raids

Weaknesses:
- Ranged attacks (low HP for its damage output)
- Keeping distance

Immunities:
- None

Behavior:
- Charges directly at players/villagers with axe
- Part of Illager patrols near Woodland Mansions
- During raids, targets villagers first
- "Johnny" variant attacks literally everything

Sounds: Villager-like grunts but menacing

Notes:
- Possibly the hardest-hitting non-boss mob in the game per hit
- Vindicator "Johnny" named variant attacks any mob — even Endermen
- The "Johnny" name is a reference to "The Shining" ("Here's Johnny!")
- Emerald drops during raids make them valuable to fight
- Second-most dangerous Illager after the Evoker in terms of raw threat

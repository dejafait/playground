Name: Stray
Type: Hostile
Category: Undead

Health: 20 HP (10 hearts)
Attack Damage: Arrow 1-4 + Slowness IV for 30 seconds (Normal/Hard)
Armor: 2
Movement Speed: 0.25
XP Dropped: 5

Spawn Locations:
- Ice Plains (Snowy Tundra)
- Ice Mountains
- Frozen Ocean
- Frozen River
- Snowy Beach
- Also spawns when a Skeleton spawns inside powder snow and freezes

Drops:
- Bones (0-2)
- Arrows (0-2)
- Tipped Arrow of Slowness (50% chance instead of regular arrows)
- Bow (8.5%)

Special Abilities:
- Shoots Tipped Arrows of Slowness — slows player movement by 60%
- Burns in sunlight (same as regular Skeleton)
- Slowness effect lasts 30 seconds on Normal, longer on Hard
- Icy/tattered appearance differentiates from regular skeletons

Weaknesses:
- Sunlight (catches fire)
- Same weaknesses as regular Skeleton

Immunities:
- Cold/freezing damage

Behavior:
- Same as regular Skeleton — strafe and shoot from range
- Targets players, iron golems, wolves
- Flees from wolves

Sounds: Same as Skeleton — rattling bones

Notes:
- Cold biome variant of the Skeleton
- Slowness arrows make it more dangerous in open combat (kites the player)
- Useful to farm for Tipped Arrows of Slowness
- Wrapped in icy/ragged cloth — has a unique visual design
- Can also spawn when a regular skeleton spawns inside powder snow

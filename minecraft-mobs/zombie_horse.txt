Name: Zombie Horse
Type: Passive (but classified as undead)
Category: Undead / Animal

Health: 15 HP (7.5 hearts)
Attack Damage: None
Armor: 0
Movement Speed: 0.20
XP Dropped: 1-3

Spawn Locations:
- Does NOT spawn naturally in Survival mode
- Only available via: Spawn Egg, /summon command, or (Bedrock) Skeleton Horse traps (very rarely)
- Java Edition: Must use commands or creative mode

Drops:
- Rotten Flesh (0-2)

Taming:
- Can be tamed via commands: /ride command or right-click with Saddle in creative
- Can be ridden with Saddle once tamed

Special Abilities:
- Same speed/jump as a standard horse (fixed stats)
- Immune to most undead effects (Smite enchantment DOES deal bonus damage)
- Does not sink in water — can walk on/through water surfaces slowly

Immunities:
- Drowning
- Does not burn in sunlight

Behavior:
- Wanders without purpose
- Ridden same as Horse after taming

Sounds: Hollow horse sounds

Notes:
- One of the most obscure mobs in the game — most players never see one in Survival
- A true survival-obtained Zombie Horse is nearly impossible without cheats
- Sometimes confused with Skeleton Horse (which DOES spawn naturally)
- The Zombie Horse is essentially a creative/command block mob
- Its undead nature means Smite enchantment deals bonus damage to it

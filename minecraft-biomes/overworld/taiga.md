# Taiga

A cold forest of spruce trees with wolves, foxes, and sweet berry bushes. Villages and outposts appear here as well.

| Field         | Value                       |
|---------------|-----------------------------|
| Dimension     | Overworld        |

| Temperature   | 0.25      |

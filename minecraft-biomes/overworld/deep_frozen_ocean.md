# Deep Frozen Ocean

The deep version of frozen ocean. Despite the name, the water is not frozen at depth. Icebergs still form and ocean monuments can appear.

## Overview

| Field         | Value                       |
|---------------|-----------------------------|
| Dimension     | Overworld        |

| Temperature   | 0.5      |
| Precipitation | Rain               |
